About this offer

BittWare’s IA-840f is an Intel Agilex®-based FPGA card designed to deliver up to 40% higher performance for data center, networking and edge compute workloads. BittWare maximized I/O features on the card using the Agilex chip’s unique tiling architecture with three QSFP-DDs (3× 200G) and PCIe Gen4 x16. The card also supports Intel® oneAPI , which enables an abstracted development flow for dramatically simplified code re-use across multiple architectures.
