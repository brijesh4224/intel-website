What’s Inside Intel-Powered Gaming Desktops

Intel-powered gaming desktops are equipped with an arsenal of technologies that elevate your gaming, streaming, and content creation.

Be ready for anything with a new architecture, enhanced overclocking, and powered-up performance on every core.

Get Future-Ready for AI1

Use AI to remove background clutter on your streams so the audience can focus on your personality and your play. Create amazing images and audio almost instantly through generative solutions. Game with unprecedented immersion, responsiveness, and realism through AI enhancements.

What makes it all possible? Intel® Core™ Ultra processors feature three different AI accelerators (CPU, GPU, and NPU) so they can handle intense AI demands with ease. That means you get all the AI-enriched capabilities you need for gaming, streaming, and creation while your desktop doesn’t even break a sweat.

Unlock Ultra Gaming Performance

Improve game performance on classic titles with a single click via ongoing updates to Intel® Application Optimization.6

Maximize Your Power Efficiency

Experience gaming at its finest—cooler, quieter, faster, and more efficient. Intel® Core™ Ultra processors deliver amazing performance with less heat, noise, and power consumption.

Megatask with Multithreading

Need to do it all at once? No problem. Intel® Core™ Ultra processors set a new standard for versatility. Reach higher levels of productivity and play through enhanced multithreading performance and specialized AI cores that empower you to game, stream, and create with minimal interruptions.

Clear the Clutter, Grab the Glory

Simplify your setup with the first-ever native Thunderbolt™ 5 technology support on a desktop processor.2 Connect all your peripherals—cameras, monitors, storage drives, and more—through a single high-speed port to eliminate cable clutter. Plus, say goodbye to lag and hello to seamless performance with blazing-fast Wi-Fi 7 compatibility or integrated Wi-Fi 6E.11

Overclock on Easy Mode

Make your gaming desktop uniquely yours with streamlined overclocking that empowers you to get more from your unlocked processor.12 Intel® Extreme Tuning Utility (Intel® XTU) brings overclocking out of the BIOS and into Windows for an easy, user-friendly experience with super granular core-by-core control.

Supercharge your top clock speed to higher than its factory limits to squeeze more performance from the same silicon without compromising system stability.
