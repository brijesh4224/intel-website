Intel Around the World

Intel employees around the globe create world-changing technology that improves the life of every person on the planet.

Our global network and manufacturing footprint provides us with the scale, capacity, and reach to establish new technologies with better quality control. We recognize that the health of local communities — especially those where our employees live and work – is critical for our shared success.
