Intel® Geti™ Software Recognized by SIA as Winner of the Best New Products & Solutions Award for Video Analytics at ISC West, 2024

LAS VEGAS, Nev. – Intel® was recognized by the Security Industry Association (SIA) as an awardee at the 2024 SIA New Products and Solutions (NPS) Awards, the flagship awards program presented in partnership with ISC West recognizing innovative security products, services and solutions.

Intel® Geti™ software was selected as the 2024 Winner of the Video Analytics for its Intel® Geti™ software and honored April 10 during an award ceremony on the Bridge Stage on the ISC West Show Floor. The product name was also displayed on the ISC West show floor throughout the event from April 9-12.

“This year’s SIA NPS Awards featured an outstanding array of new products and solutions in the security industry, and the judges were impressed by the 2024 pool of entrants,” said Josh Heisler, chair of the SIA NPS Awards Committee and global head of tech and tools at Amazon. “Following an extensive process of technology demonstrations and panel-driven judging, we were honored to select the 2024 awardees, and we congratulate all the winners.”

“The SIA NPS Program is the most recognized global platform for launching new products each year. SIA applauds the 2024 SIA NPS Awards honorees of this year’s highly competitive program,” said SIA CEO Don Erickson. “Thanks to the always-thorough consideration of the judges, the SIA NPS Awards is the most trusted awards program for new security technologies, and earning recognition in the NPS awards is a major accomplishment.”

About Intel® Geti™ Software

Intel Geti software is a collaborative, intuitive solution where data scientists, developers, and domain experts can train deep learning models for computer vision applications.

With Intel Geti software, users build computer vision AI models rapidly and with less data. The product eases laborious data labeling, model training, and optimization tasks across the AI model development process, empowering teams to produce custom AI models at scale.

Vision AI models users developed can be leveraged in a wide array of applications such as defect detection in the factory floor, automated checkout in retail shops, intersection monitoring for cities, and much more.

Learn more about Intel Geti software: Intel® Geti™ Software – Intel’s Computer Vision AI Platform

Intel Geti software is part of Intel® Tiber™ Edge Platform, a new solution enabling enterprises to develop, deploy, run, and manage edge applications, AI, and infrastructure at scale with cloud-like simplicity.

About SIA

Since its inception in 1979, SIA NPS has been the security industry’s premier product awards program. New products are reviewed by a panel of judges with extensive industry experience, and in 2024, following significant deliberations, the 31 judges presented awards for technologies covering 30 product and service categories.
