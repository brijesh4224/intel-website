The ever increasing advancement in process technology makes power consumption a major concern with today’s FPGAs. To combat this concern, Intel® FPGA provides PowerPlay power analysis and optimization tools. Power analysis tools accurately estimate the power of your design from the concept stages to implementation. Push-button power optimization technology further assists in reducing power for your design.
