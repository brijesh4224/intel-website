Get to Know the PS Series of Intel® Core™ Ultra Processors

Built-in Intel Arc GPU and NPU in a Socketed Processors

The PS Series of Intel® Core™ Ultra processors, with multiple compute engines increase edge AI capabilities, including a built-in Intel® Arc™ GPU2 and an integrated NPU called Intel® AI Boost. And a versatile, LGA socket-based SoC elevates edge innovation, with full performance at 65 watts.

Incredible AI and Graphics for the Edge

Enable edge AI and graphics in more places. From Generative AI augmented experience in retail and hospitality to AI-assisted workflows in health and life science.

Intel® Xeon® 6 Processors with E-cores for Networking and Edge Product Brief

Deliver new levels of performance and efficiency with the power-efficient Intel® Core™ Ultra processors. These processors feature stunning graphics and advanced AI in a simplified system-on-chip (SoC) LGA package to support your demanding edge use cases. The LGA packaging enables faster time to market for build-to-order edge systems.
