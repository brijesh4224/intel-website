A Culture of Excellence

Our dynamic workplace is built on execution excellence, results-orientation, and a relentless drive toward continued innovation.

Sourcing Minerals Responsibly

We continue to expand our efforts to map our supply chain to determine the sources of critical minerals used in our products. A key technology industry initiative in our RISE strategy is to significantly expand our impact in responsible minerals and accelerate the creation of new sourcing standards.

Champions of Accessibility

Through our integrated approach to accessibility, we unlock technology to create equitable computing experiences for all. In our workplace, we strive to become a global employer of choice for people with disabilities and those caring for family members with disabilities.

10.2 Billion Gallons of Water Saved

In 2023, we conserved approximately 10.2 billion gallons of water in our operations and community partnerships and enabled restoration of about 3.1 billion gallons through watershed restoration projects.

21.6 Million Hours of Service

We create opportunities for our employees to share their technology expertise and other skills with our communities. Since we launched the Intel Involved volunteer program in 1995, our employees have generously donated more than 21.6 million hours of service.

Built-In Energy Efficiency

With each new generation of products, Intel aims to offer higher performance and improved energy efficiency compared to previous generations, thereby reducing the Scope 3 greenhouse gas emissions of our products in customer applications and overall energy consumption.

See how we are helping to make computing sustainable
Renewable Electrical Vehicle Charging

Intel collaborated with Heysoft Pvt. Ltd. to develop a system that enables electric vehicle chargers to prioritize renewables-based energy when available. The collaboration is one of some 370 Intel RISE Technology Initiative (IRTI) projects that we have funded in 42 countries cumulatively through 2023, using Intel® technology to help solve unique challenges that affect individuals and organizations globally.

See how IRTI is activating technology as a force for good

Reducing Our Carbon Footprint

In 2022, we announced our goal to achieve net-zero greenhouse gas emissions (Scope 1 and 2) across our global operations by 2040. In 2023, we took that goal a step further and committed to work with our value chain to achieve net-zero upstream Scope 3 greenhouse gas emissions by 2050.
