About this offer

FA927S is a full height, half length, PCIe Add-In acceleration card designed by Flyslice, equipped with the latest Intel Agilex® 7 SoC FPGA I-Series device, offering 2.7M LEs resources, transceiver rates up to 116 Gbps, PCIe 5.0 and Computing Express Link (CXL) support. FA927S combines a number of high-end hardware interfaces, including PCI Express 5.0 x16 golden finger, two 100G QSFP-28 connectors and two DDR4-2400 memory channels up to 16GB capacity, to provide excellent computing power and high bandwidth. With these features, FA927S is ideal for bandwidth-intensive and time-critical acceleration applications, such as financial trading, networking storage, artificial intelligence, etc.
