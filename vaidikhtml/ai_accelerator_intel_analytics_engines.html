Accelerate Business Outcomes with Integrated Analytics Acceleration

Simplify your data analytics pipeline using Intel® Analytics Engines integrated into Intel® Xeon® Scalable processors. These built-in technologies accelerate analytics and database operations so you get insights faster—without needing additional CPU cores or discrete accelerators.

How Your Business Wins with Intel® Analytics Engines

Make Better Business Decisions

Get insights faster. Intel® Analytics Engines process more queries per second so you can understand your customer better, manage risk more effectively, and create new revenue streams and cross-selling opportunities.

Cut Costs and Complexity

Simplify your data pipeline. Intel® Analytics Engines offer acceleration in a single architecture so you don’t need to invest in unnecessary hardware.

Improve Energy Efficiency

Maximize performance while using less power. Intel® Xeon® Scalable processors offload resource-intensive tasks from the CPU to built-in accelerators, improving overall utilization and saving energy.

Explore Integrated Intel® Analytics Engine Accelerators

Integrated analytics acceleration included in Intel® Xeon® Scalable processors maximizes performance for your most demanding workloads, from on-premises database servers to advanced cloud-based workloads.

Database Acceleration

Intel® In-Memory Analytics Accelerator (Intel® IAA)

Accelerate compression and decompression and reduce large memory footprints. Intel® IAA delivers fast data transfers and more transactions per second for in-memory databases, open source databases, and data stores.

Data Movement Acceleration

Intel® Data Streaming Accelerator (Intel® DSA)

Optimize performance for storage, networking, and data-intensive workloads. Intel® DSA quickly moves data across CPU, memory, and external I/O devices by accelerating memory-to-memory data transfers and read/write operations.

Learn how Intel® DSA works

Data Encryption and Compression

Intel® QuickAssist Technology (Intel® QAT)

Store, process, and move more data while cutting storage costs. By accelerating data compression/decompression and encryption, Intel® QAT can help speed up database backups and improve overall performance in networking and storage applications.
