Accelerate Diagnosis and Discovery and Improve Quality of Life Everywhere with Intel® AI

Growing and aging populations, novel diseases, and worker shortages present steep challenges to healthcare and life sciences industries. Fortunately, breakthrough AI technology can accelerate the pace of discovery and empower digital hospitals to succeed. With a global ecosystem of partners, Intel offers a comprehensive AI portfolio to help healthcare and research organizations deploy AI that’s more secure, sustainable, scalable, and interoperable to help improve the lives of every person on the planet.

Deploy Open Source AI Efficiently and with Less Risk

Run Inferencing on Existing Intel® Hardware

Deploy the latest AI solutions for healthcare and life sciences on your existing Intel® hardware. From GPUs to CPUs, Intel-enabled platforms are cost-effective and high performance and can help you meet stringent sustainability, security, and compliance requirements.

Help Safeguard Patient Data with Robust Cybersecurity

Strong cybersecurity starts at the chip level. Intel-enabled solutions feature built-in capabilities to support confidential computing and harden platforms against breaches and ransomware.

Navigate an End-to-End Ecosystem of Vendors

Intel collaborates with vendors at all levels to optimize AI performance. You benefit from access to a complete, end-to-end portfolio of devices, hardware, and software.
