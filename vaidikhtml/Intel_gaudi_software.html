Intel® Gaudi® Software

Intel Gaudi software, like our accelerator hardware, was purpose-designed to deliver Gen AI performance, efficiency, and, most importantly, increased developer ease of use and productivity. With support for go-to frameworks like PyTorch and DeepSpeed, and popular LLM and multimodal models supported on Hugging Face, the goal of Intel Gaudi software is to enable you to easily enable new models or migrate existing code with 3 to 5 lines of code. In essence, Intel Gaudi software and its many tools and documents are designed to meet deep learning developers where you are — enabling you to develop what and how you want.
