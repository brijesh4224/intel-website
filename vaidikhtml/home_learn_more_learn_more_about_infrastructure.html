A Leap Forward for Edge Infrastructure

Leveraging deep edge experience, Intel® Tiber™ Edge Platform gives you everything you need to build, deploy, and manage scalable edge infrastructure and applications. It includes built-in AI runtime for policy-based automation, streamlines AI management, and optimizes workload deployment and orchestration. It also allows you to implement new solutions without ripping and replacing.

Get More Out of the Edge with End-to-End Management Capabilities

Implement Innovative Edge Solutions on Existing Infrastructure

Intel® Tiber™ Edge Platform supports diverse architectures and applications and enables new solutions on greenfield or brownfield components. It offers dynamic workload placement based on policies while helping you overcome connectivity challenges and efficiencies between near and far edges. It also automates cluster orchestration to centralize control and meet application requirements.

Streamline Edge Solution Management

From day zero through days one, two, and beyond, Intel® Tiber™ Edge Platform offers streamlined solution management and enables custom hybrid edge implementations. Deep telemetry enables policy-based lifecycle management for edge node fleets and gives you a cloud-like, single-pane-of-glass view of your deployments. AI runtime is built into the edge node software to enable policy-based automation of lifecycle management tasks.

Powerful Tools to Manage AI Models and Applications

Streamline the deployment of AI across the edge node fleet with optimizations and tools that allow you to tackle challenging performance, latency, and other requirements, including MLOps for ongoing model updates.

Extra Protection for Challenging Edge Conditions

Safeguard data with consistent, hardened edge security measures that enable full disk encryption, local attestation, boot management, and zero-trust security environments.
