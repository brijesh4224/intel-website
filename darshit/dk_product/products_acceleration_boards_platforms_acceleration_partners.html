Altera® FPGA AI NICs and SmartNICs

Altera FPGA AI NICs and SmartNICs are network adapter cards with hardware programmable accelerators and Ethernet connectivity that can accelerate infrastructure applications running on the host CPU.

The portfolio of Intel and Intel Partner FPGA-acceleration platforms includes SmartNICs, Infrastructure Processing Units (IPUs), and System on Modules (SoMs). Designed for use in telecommunications, cloud service provider, and other types of enterprise data center environments, these acceleration boards and platform designs are continuously being qualified and sold by leading original equipment manufacturer (OEM) server providers.
