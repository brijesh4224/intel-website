Make the Business Case for Open and Virtualized RAN

Discover how an open and virtualized radio access network (RAN) improves network agility, with cloud-like resource optimization and scalability. You’ll also see how virtualized RAN (vRAN) helps to optimize power use and provides a platform for new services.

In this paper, you will learn:

How vRAN provides a platform for innovation.

How the virtualization of hardware and network functions enables resources to be optimized.

How energy requirements in the RAN can be reduced using pooling, artificial intelligence and processor-level power states.

How you can deploy vRAN, including a roadmap for implementation.
