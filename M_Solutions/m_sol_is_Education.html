Help All Students Reach Their Full Potential with Smart, Secure, Innovative Technology

Technology is a critical component for teaching and learning at all levels of education—from kindergarten to middle school through high school, college, and beyond. When integrated seamlessly and thoughtfully both inside the classroom and at home, technology not only makes the lives of educators and families easier but engages and motivates students while preparing them for the future.

As an active education supporter and trusted industry partner, Intel works closely with everyone involved in the educational ecosystem to help shape the future of education technology, drive innovation in education, and deliver equitable EdTech solutions that meet the needs of all students—no matter how or where they learn.

Discover What Intel Offers for Your Education Needs

We’re all working toward the same goal: maximizing educational outcomes so students can reach their full potential. To achieve this, every member of the education community— students, parents, educators, staff, administrators, and IT—needs the right combination of empowering technologies and tools that create smooth, secure learning environments and experiences.
