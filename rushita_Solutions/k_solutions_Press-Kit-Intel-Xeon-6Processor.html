Press Kit: Intel Xeon 6 Processors

Intel Xeon 6 processors are designed with high core counts and built-in acceleration for multiple segments to address a broad array of demanding workloads.

Press Kit

January 27, 2025

Contact Intel PR

Follow Intel Newsroom on social:

More Data Center & HPC News

Crafted for artificial intelligence (AI), high performance computing (HPC), networking, storage, database management and security, Intel® Xeon® 6 processors deliver new technologies and capabilities across a range of applications. Through an open systems approach and based on the x86 computer architecture, Intel enables its customers and partners to take advantage of the opportunities provided by AI on many workloads. 

Xeon 6 with Performance-cores is the best head node for AI training with dedicated accelerators1, also delivering uncompromised performance for AI on central processing units, traditional enterprise apps and HPC. Xeon 6 with Efficient-cores provides best-in-class power efficiency and lower total cost of ownership for cloud-native and telco workloads1.
