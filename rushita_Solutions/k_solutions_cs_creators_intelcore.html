Features and Performance

With AI-enabled, high clock speeds, performance and efficiency cores, plus more cache, users can game, stream, edit, video chat, browse—or just take care of business—faster and better than ever.

Delivers the power to stay ahead with up to 6 GHz clock speed, plus Intel Wi-Fi 6E (Gig+)—and Intel® Wi-Fi 7 (5 Gig) enabled on select processors—for outstanding Wi-Fi performance, improved latency, and fast memory I/O with support for DDR4/DDR5 and PCI 4.0/5.0.

High-performance cores and optimized platform features will keep users more productive than ever. Plus, they’ll look and sound their best with videoconferencing with supported AI features, including noise cancellation, lighting correction, and background blur.

With Intel® Graphics integrated into select CPUs, users can now edit and create in stunning 8K HDR quality, enjoy streaming 4K UHD content, power virtual reality, and play the latest games. And for gamers and designers who want to take visual realism to a whole other level? Add Intel® Arc™ high-performance graphics for pro-level gaming and creating.
