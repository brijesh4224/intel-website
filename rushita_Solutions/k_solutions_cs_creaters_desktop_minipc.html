Mini PCs

Mini PCs are full-capability computers small enough to tuck away in a drawer. The performance of Intel® Core™ processors and large local storage let Mini PCs handle complex tasks while keeping a low profile at home or at work

Benefits of a Mini PC

Replace the chunky tower with a PC that runs the apps and software that you love. The stylish design has large, local storage, and supports apps like Microsoft Office, Skype, Adobe Creative Cloud, and more.1

Mini PCs with Intel® processor bring full PC capability to small spaces, offering wireless connections, faster meeting starts, and power to drive multiple displays and digital signage. Get big results in a tiny form factor.

Mini PCs use less power than average full size desktop towers2, which lets you free up budget for other priorities without compromising sky-high performance. Save money. Perform better.

Mini PC room Mini PCs powered by Intel provide features to expand your media capabilities. Premium 4K video, Intel® Ready Mode Technology (Intel® RMT), software compatibility, and large expandable storage let you work, study, play, create, and entertain in any room.

From large classrooms to small learning locations, give your students the performance and space they need to succeed. Bring the power of education to the smallest of places with Intel®-based mini PCs.
