About this offer

The board is based on an Intel Agilex® I-Series SoC.The main features of the board are: Intel Agilex® I-Series SoC FPGA ⸱ Quad Core ARM® Cortex® A53 ⸱ 2700 KLE ⸱ PCIe Gen5 x16, CXL™ EP compatible ⸱ 400GbE (8 x 53 Gbps)⸱ 3x DDR4 banks⸱ 4x HSI connectors (2x PCIe Gen5 x16 EP/RP, 1x CXL™ EP compatible). The target markets are as wide as Security SmartNIC, Memory expansion & storage, and Networking Switch.
