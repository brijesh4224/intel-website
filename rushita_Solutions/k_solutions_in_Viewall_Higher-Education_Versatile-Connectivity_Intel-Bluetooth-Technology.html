How Does Bluetooth® Technology Work?

Bluetooth® short-range wireless radio technology operates on the 2.4 GHz frequency band. Bluetooth technology allows PCs and mobile devices to sync with peripherals such as wireless headphones, keyboards, or mice without requiring cables or supporting network infrastructure.
