Intel® Windows DCH Graphics Drivers FAQ

Content Type: Product Information & Documentation | Article ID: 000059048 | Last Reviewed: 02/11/2025

Microsoft has moved to a new Universal Drivers architecture that started with Windows® 10 October 2018 Update. This change resulted in a shift of our Intel® Graphics Drivers starting with Intel Graphics driver 26.20.100.7985. See the below topics for information on how this might impact your system.
