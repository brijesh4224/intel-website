Reliable Stability

Maintain your fleet, scale with confidence, and transition OS generations on your terms. Rigorous testing of the various hardware components helps ensure that all brands of PCs powered by Intel® Core™ Ultra processors and built on Intel vPro® Enterprise1 deliver a professional-grade experience that’s reliable and seamless.

Upgrading end-user systems in your enterprise can be daunting. Intel® Stable IT Platform Program (Intel® SIPP), available with Intel vPro Enterprise, addresses common upgrade challenges, including driver variations, image management complexity, and operating system transitions to help you transition to new technologies at your own pace with assured quality and performance.

One Integrated and Validated Platform

Intel vPro® delivers Intel’s latest PC platform technologies in one integrated and validated solution. The quality of design, long-term reliability, and compatibility of devices powered by Intel vPro is assured by one of the most rigorous validation processes in the industry. Intel works hand in hand with OEMs to certify that devices are built to give IT and end users the stability and reliability of a true business-class device.

Helping Ensure Business Continuity

Intel® SIPP features an extensive validation program that aims for no hardware changes throughout the buying cycle for at least 15 months or until the next generational release.

With fewer hardware changes, users experience fewer hassles and interruptions for a seamless experience overall—especially for PC setup and configuration and image deployment.

In addition, Intel vPro® provides full corporate platform validation for multiple concurrent Windows releases. This helps businesses better manage OS transitions, such as Windows 10 to Windows 11, and take advantage of extended support from Microsoft for any given OS release.

A Track Record of Trust

Intel® SIPP has delivered and defined high-quality components annually for over a decade. As computing increases in complexity, the scope and standards of Intel's performance experience also continues to advance. By extending Intel® SIPP's commitment to an even larger set of technologies—Ethernet, wireless connectivity, Thunderbolt™ 4—Intel® technology comprises the critical majority of many device foundations today.
