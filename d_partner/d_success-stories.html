Partner Chat Series

The Edge Innovation Partner Chat Videos is a series of 10-minute discussions between Bill Pearson, Vice President of IOTG and General Manager of Developer Enabling, and an executive-level representative of an ecosystem partner. The goal of the video is to showcase Intel’s Ecosystem Partners, their relationship with Intel, and how Intel products and technology help ecosystem partners win at the edge.
