Your opportunity at Intel

Find your path at Intel with wide-ranging opportunities to learn and grow.

You worked hard to hone your expertise. Now, you’re searching for a place where you can put all your efforts to use at a job that satisfies your passion and curiosity. At Intel, you’ll discover endless opportunities. From business to engineering and beyond, for those just starting out to those with advanced experience, Intel can help you make the most of your career.

Advance your knowledge with internal and external programs like tuition assistance for job-related degrees or coursework. Get timely, personalized support from an expert advisor, mentor or coach who can help you with career progression, resume writing and networking, skill-building and much more. Enrich your career and explore other areas of interest by participating in a variety of short- and long-term assignments—all without ever leaving Intel.

To help you prepare for the road ahead, Intel offers a total compensation package that ranks among the best in the industry, consisting of competitive pay, stock, bonuses, and benefit programs. Eligible employees can purchase stock at a discounted price, and we grant Restricted Stock Units to employees at all levels. We also offer competitive retirement programs and Identity Protection to protect your financial goals.

Flexible work life

At Intel, flexibility extends to how employees get work done, balance their work lives and pursue both their career and personal goals.

To foster work experiences that spark the connection, innovation and purpose needed to deliver results, Intel offers three work models: hybrid, onsite and remote. We value flexibility in most of our job roles, and we provide opportunities and environments where employees can collaborate to do their best work. Moreover, select manufacturing employees can work a compressed week—or work nights and earn up to a 16% differential.

Based on the role and the needs of the business, our flexible work options empower employees to do their best work and create a culture of connection while fostering innovation.

Read more about our hybrid-first model.

Diversity, equity and inclusion

Our aim is to continue expanding opportunities for our employees and the industry through technology, inclusion and digital readiness initiatives.

Diversity, equity and inclusion have long been core to Intel’s values and instrumental to driving innovation and delivering strong business growth. We are advancing diversity, equity, accessibility and inclusion in our global workforce and advocating for public policies and laws that improve the lives of our employees and our communities. We are actively advancing toward our 2030 goals, which include doubling the number of women and underrepresented minorities in senior leadership and increasing the representation of women in technical roles to 40%.

Our nearly 40 Employee Resource Groups (ERGs) reflect how Intel tries to include and empower every employee to embrace a sense of belonging. Intel has ERGs organized around shared attributes and goals, and they are open to anyone and everyone, with the opportunity to opt in or out at every employee’s discretion.

To learn more about our path to creating a more inclusive and responsible workforce, industry and world, check out Intel’s Corporate Responsibility report.

Best-in-class fertility benefits

Starting or expanding your family is an exciting time in your life, and Intel is proud to support you through the adventure and potential complexity. With best-in-class fertility benefits worth $40k and up to $15k reimbursement for adoption expenses with no lifetime cap on the number of adoptions, you can focus on growing your family in the way that works for you.

We also recognize how difficult and stressful it can be for a new parent to return to work after welcoming a child. The New Parent Reintegration (NPR) program allows employees to temporarily modify their work schedule when returning from Intel Paid Bonding Leave.

Read about Easvari and Beth’s experiences.

Prepare for your children's future

As part of our commitment to lifelong learning, we believe in helping our employees’ children prepare for a solid future. That’s why we have programs dedicated to tutoring, college coaching and test prep classes. We also offer the Andy Grove Scholarship for Kids program, open to employees worldwide, which can be used towards tuition and related costs, such as books or fees.

Learn more aboutour Scholarship program.

LGBT+ offerings

Intel offers a variety of inclusive LGBT+ offerings, such as covering all gender reassignment or reaffirming procedures recognized as medically necessary by the World Professional Association for Transgender Health. In the U.S., Intel helps ease the financial burden of expanding your family by offering up to $15k reimbursement for qualified adoption expenses per adoption. And our IGLOBE ERG drives a safe, inclusive and productive work community for all employees regardless of their sexual orientation, gender identity or gender expression.

Environmental sustainability

Driving to the lowest possible environmental footprint as we grow helps us create efficiencies and respond to the needs of our stakeholders. We invest in conservation projects and set company-wide environmental targets to drive reductions in greenhouse gas emissions, energy use, water use and waste generation. We also work with others to increase our “handprint,” or how Intel® technologies can help others reduce their footprints. We committed to net-zero greenhouse gas emissions in our global operations by 2040.

Find more information onIntel's environmental sustainability efforts.

Social impact

Changing the future of the world also means supporting the communities we serve. Intel and the Intel Foundation have a strong legacy of partnering with community organizations, governments and academia to catalyze action. This includes creating positive impacts on global social challenges, broadening equitable access and opportunity to STEM education, and supporting community needs to inspire the next generation of innovators. Working with the US National Science Foundation, we made a $100 million investment over the next decade to establish education and research collaborations with universities, community colleges and technical educators in Ohio and nationwide.

Take a break

Your mental and physical well-being are important. We designed our programs and benefits to support both.

Your wellness matters. On top of paid time off, employees can take a 4-week or 8-week sabbatical after eligible lengths of service to disconnect from work so they can come back refreshed and ready to do wonderful. Additionally, through Intel benefits, our employees and their families get exclusive discounts and travel perks to make time away from work more affordable and enjoyable.

Watch and learn more about Intel’s Sabbatical benefit1.

We also offer various apps to support your emotional well-being, such as CALM, Modern Health, Headspace and Sleepio. Additional support is available 24/7 through our Employee Assistance Program. Build resilience, manage stress and get personal training, physical therapy, nutrition, weight loss assistance and more through our myStrength and Intel Vitality programs.

Read how Dana used Modern Health to overcome pandemic anxiety.
