Taking a Tech-Supported, Skills-Based Approach to K‒12 Education

Students will soon be expected to use skill sets and mindsets such as critical thinking, problem solving, social and emotional skills, and design thinking to succeed in the Fourth Industrial Revolution.

We’re here to help educators and administrators prepare students for this future by providing a comprehensive collection of professional development (PD) programs, educational resources, K‒12 technologies, and partner solutions.

As a long-standing education ally, we’re invested in helping educators learn how to best integrate technology-based activities into existing curricula, everyday teaching, and learning environments to help foster the innovative thinking and technical skills students need for future success.

Find Everything You Need to Create Tech-Infused Learning Experiences

Our portfolio of PD programs, education technology (edtech) tools, and K‒12 technologies can help you quickly adapt and create learning experiences where students can experiment, think differently, and build higher-order thinking and technical skills.
