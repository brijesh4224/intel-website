Bandwidth-intensive applications and workloads and reduced implementation costs equal tangible benefits for moving to 25 and 100 Gigabit Ethernet.

Intel® Ethernet 800 Series

The 800 Series products offer speeds of 25 and 100GbE that are also backwards compatible to 10GbE. This series implements Dynamic Device Personalization (DDP) for a fully programmable packet-processing pipeline and supports RDMA (RoCEv2 and iWARP) for low-latency, high-throughput workloads.
