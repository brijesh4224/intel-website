Intel is committed to earning customers’ trust and enabling their success for all our products and services. This means our investment in quality includes collaborating with partners and customers as each day presents new opportunities and challenges amid a rapidly evolving and increasingly data-centric world. We are committed to being responsive, accountable, and transparent.

We recognize our work is never done, but together with our partners and customers, we will continuously learn and build quality into everything we do.

Help be a Voice of Quality (VoQ) for Intel by reviewing the ‘Latest Quality Papers’.

We passionately believe that quality is fundamental, and that:

  • Our customers come first. We listen and collaborate to deliver world-class quality and an outstanding customer experience.
  • We build safety, security, reliability, compliance, and quality into our products, technologies, services, and throughout our company.
  • We deliver innovative products that support and enable data integrity, system availability, and configurability for our expanding digital and connected world.
  • Our employees embody a quality discipline and holistically drive continuous improvement, prevention, and closed-loop learning.

Pat Gelsinger, Intel Chief Executive Officer
