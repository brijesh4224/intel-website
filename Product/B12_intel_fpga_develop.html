Altera® FPGA Development Kits

Intel offers a wide selection of development kits to accelerate the FPGA design process. Includes general purpose FPGA and SoC development kits in tabletop and PCIe form factors. Comes with a variety of connector interfaces for transceivers and FPGA workloads to meet application needs. HPS enablement features and options are supported. Covers Agilex™ FPGA, Stratix® FPGA, Arria® FPGA, Cyclone® FPGA , and MAX® FPGA Development Kits.
