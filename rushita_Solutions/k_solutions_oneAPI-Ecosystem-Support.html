A

  • Adobe

    • Creators demand the fastest and most reliable technology to stay in their creative flow. Intel and Adobe provide the tools that meet these demands. The combined power of Adobe Sensei and Intel's AI technologies keeps creators moving at the speed of light. Whether video editing with Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe After Effects, or 3D texturing the next blockbuster movie with Adobe Substance Painter, Adobe and Intel have the combined experience to provide creative tools with performance driven hardware.

  • Autodesk

    • Intel partners closely with Autodesk, a leader in 3D design, engineering, and entertainment software, to enable the latest technologies and optimizations on Autodesk applications, allowing users to work better, faster, and smarter.

  • Blackmagic Design

    • Blackmagic invests in a strong relationship with Intel to create the best experience on their software and hardware products.

  • Blender

    • Blender is the free and open-source 3D creation suite. It supports the entirety of the 3D pipeline—modeling, rigging, animation, simulation, rendering, compositing and motion tracking, video editing, and 2D animation pipeline. Intel has worked very closely with Blender to optimize the software and give end-users an optimal experience on Intel-based devices. Intel is also a gold sponsor of the Blender Foundation.

  • CyberLink

    • CyberLink is the premier developer of multimedia editing software with over 200 patented technologies. With the award-winning Director software family, CyberLink provides complete video, photo, and audio editing solutions for creators of all skill levels and across all devices. CyberLink PowerDirector’s video editing tools work seamlessly with Intel® CPUs and GPUs to unleash new levels of performance and efficiency with Intel® Deep Link technology. Experience unmatched hardware acceleration, whether you’re using advanced editing tools like AI Style Transfer or publishing 8K videos, with lightning-fast rendering speeds.

  • D5 Render

    • The D5 Render is a real-time ray tracing rendering software for architects, 3D artists, designers, and students. With D5, you can fully dive into the creative moment, sculpting with light and truly playing in the scenes you create. Over 8000 models and 2000 PBR materials are ready-to-use in the built-in library, including dynamic models, particles, vegetation, Animals, vehicles, etc. Works in conjunction with Trimble SketchUp, Autodesk 3ds Max, Autodesk Revit, GRAPHISOFT Archicad, Rhinoceros 3D, Blender, and Maxon Cinema 4D.

  • FFmpeg

    • FFmpeg is one of the most popular open-source multimedia frameworks, which include a library of tools for decode, encode, transcode, mux, demux, stream, filter, and play various multimedia formats. Today, FFmpeg is used in many well-known software tools, like VLC and HandBrake. The FFmpeg community and Intel® Software developers have worked for many years to provide support for Intel® Graphics in the FFmpeg tools using Intel® Media SDK tools and Intel® Quick Sync Video technology. Most recently, the work has been done to include support of the first generation of Intel® Iris® Xe MAX discrete graphics card, including AV1 HW encoding.

  • Foundry

    • Foundry is a developer of creative software for the digital design and media & entertainment industries. The company’s product and engineering teams have been working closely with Intel for the last several years, optimizing Foundry’s software development process to take advantage of the latest innovations in Intel’s hardware and software performance. Recent collaborations included scalability and multithreading in Katana, rendering improvements in Modo, and optimizations with Nuke to improve performance with higher thread-count CPUs.

  • Handbrake

    • HandBrake is a free and open-source transcoder for digital video files that converts media files from nearly any format to a selection of modern, widely supported codecs. HandBrake and Intel developers worked together closely to integrate Intel® Deep Link technology and AV1 encoding capabilities into the latest software offering.

  • Magix

    • For 25 years, MAGIX has been creating cutting-edge software reaching a global audience for the creation, design, presentation, and archiving of digital photos, graphics, websites, videos, and music. Intel is a premium partner, sharing a joint mission to provide optimal performance. This partnership provides compelling solutions based on state-of-the-art fundamentals like hardware-accelerated video encoding and decoding. It also allows for integration of the latest innovations like artificial intelligence, machine learning, and HDR video.

  • Nero

    • Nero creates software that helps consumers around the world to simply enjoy their videos, photos, and music. Their portfolio of multimedia software includes powerful applications for media management, video editing/converting/playback, and content syncing. The Nero AI has undergone significant development with Intel, now taking full advantage of Intel® CPUs and GPUs. The AI Photo Tagger now recognizes more subjects and works more precisely and intuitively. Nero MediaHome now supports playback of HDR videos.

  • Skylum

    • Skylum is the software developer of the highly awarded Luminar series image editor apps. The goal is to give photographers and creatives the best photo software for editing, managing, and making beautiful photos. Intel and Skylum work together to provide an excellent experience on Intel® processor-based devices. Much of this optimization work has been focused on the AI/ML features in Luminar, which now takes full advantage of Intel® Deep Learning Boost present in modern processors.

  • Topaz Labs

    • Advancements in machine learning have opened up many opportunities to provide intelligent photo and video enhancements. Topaz Labs believes that by leveraging the deep learning power of AI, they can deliver remarkable objective image and video quality improvements tailored to the individualized needs of your media. By combining the depth of Topaz Labs’ AI models and Intel’s processing power, photographers and videographers can quickly and intelligently remove distracting noise, add natural sharpening, upscale up to 600% without loss of quality, and perform the myriad tasks needed to truly improve their photos and videos.

  • Wondershare

    • Creativity is in the Wondershare DNA. Their mission is to empower digital creators with software solutions tailored to their imagination. They redefine the possibilities of digital experiences via game-changing innovations to shape the next generation of storytelling across millions of users in over 150 countries. Intel is a strategic partner, collaborating on cutting-edge technologies such as media and artificial intelligence, and providing a smooth end-to-end workflow experience on PC

  • XSplit

    • SplitmediaLabs (XSplit) has for over 10 years provided software to give the best user experience for both streaming and recording from your computer. Software includes XSplit Broadcaster, Presenter, and VCam. Intel and XSplit have worked together for many years with the goal of utilizing Intel’s hardware to the outmost. For example, Xsplit´s software utilizes Intel® Quick Sync Video for hardware video encoding, as well as Intel® Deep Learning Boost for fast and efficient machine learning inferencing.
