Founded in 2002, Intel Labs is a global research organization focused on pioneering transformative solutions for every person on the planet. We collaborate across the industry to solve the most significant computing challenges in the future and then ready those solutions to scale and improve the lives of every person on Earth.

A Broad Research Scope

Intel Labs research spans topics of artificial intelligence, new computing architectures, cloud to edge infrastructure, security, privacy, programming and design, and much more. In the videos below, you'll hear leaders from across Intel Labs discuss the amazing research happening in their areas of expertise, and how that research will have an ongoing impact on the future of innovation.
