Master of Performance and Efficiency

Get to the core of multitasking.

Meet the cores ready to give you power where you need it most.

Bringing together performance cores and efficient cores, Intel® Core™ processor1 (14th Gen) is a multitasking master. Letting performance cores take on the big tasks like gameplay and editing, while efficient cores give you room to breathe by picking off the smaller tasks in the background. And thanks to Intel® Thread Director2, you can always transition seamlessly between tasks without losing any power.

Do More With Less

Get your hands on big performance from slimline hardware.

With increased IPC, up to four SSDs, 192 GB of RAM, up to 32 TB of storage, and a turbo clock speed of 5.5 GHz, you can breeze through smoother gameplay, bigger performance, and less lag, all without relying too much on the cloud.

Performance Multiplied

With Intel Thread Director never feel fixed to any one task or place.

Intel Thread Director® makes sure the right Thread is always in the right Core, so you can switch between tasks with ease, and always keep power where you need it. Giving you the confidence to switch up your workload and take on even more.

Product and Performance Information

1 Performance hybrid architecture combines two core microarchitectures, Performance-cores (P-cores) and Efficient-cores (E-cores), on a single processor. Select 13th Gen Intel® Core™ processors do not have performance hybrid architecture, only P-cores, and have same cache size as prior generation; see ark.intel.com for SKU details.

2 Built into the hardware, Intel® Thread Director is provided only in performance hybrid architecture configurations of newer Intel® Core™ Ultra processors; OS enablement is required. Available features and functionality vary by OS.

3 Up to 75% Latency reduction: Is based on Intel simulation data (79%) of 802.11ax with and without OFDMA using 9 clients. Average latency without OFDM is 36ms, with OFDMA average latency is reduced to 7.6ms. Latency improvement requires that the 802.11ax (Wi-Fi 6) router and all clients support OFDMA.

4 Intel® Arc™ GPUs only available on select Intel® Core™ Ultra H-series processor-powered systems with at least 16GB of system memory in dual channel configuration. OEM enablement required; check with OEM or retailer for system configuration details.

5 Visit intel.com/performanceindex for workloads and configurations. Results may vary.

6 Performance varies by use, configuration, and other factors. Learn more at www.Intel.com/PerformanceIndex.
