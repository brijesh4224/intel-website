Bringing New Levels of Service with Interactive Kiosks

Interactive kiosks enable rich, personalized, interactive customer experiences to enhance in-branch visits and power new and improved levels of customer service and business growth. In addition, self-check-in kiosks enable customers to book meetings and queue for transaction services, freeing staff to provide customer assistance throughout the branch. This will also help manage staffing levels and increase customer retention.

Captivating and Engaging Digital Signage

Digital signs enable retailers to provide personalized promotions based on anonymous demographics of nearby customers. Smart digital signage not only provides valuable information on a bank's new offerings, but allows these messages to be customized to the customer's age group, ethnicity or gender by region or time of day. Leveraging a cloud-based content management platform provides a high level of efficiency and flexibility to scale-up quickly to numerous locations and digital sign sizes.
