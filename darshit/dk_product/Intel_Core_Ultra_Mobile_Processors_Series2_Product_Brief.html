Intel® Core™ Ultra Processors (HX-SKUs)

Enthusiast Level Performance at Lower Power Expanding AI Across the Platform Maximized Connectivity
Built for uncompromised performance that lasts using up to 8 P-cores and up to 16 E-cores that will free laptop enthusiasts from their power cables. Intel® Core™ Ultra processors (HX-SKUs) give gamers, creators, and professionals enthusiasts level performance for consuming and creating on the go. At the heart of the AI PC lies an NPU for running AI tasks locally. Intel® Core™ Ultra processors (HX-SKUs) feature up to 13 TOPS3 of performance. This NPU handles generative AI tools and AI assistants locally so users can continue working with and without outside connections. For larger, more demanding AI tasks, Intel® Core™ Ultra processors (HX-SKUs) can easily scale up to 36 TOPS3 by utilizing the built-in CPU, GPU, and NPUs. Equipped with the latest in connectivity for maximizing setups, Intel® Core™ Ultra processors (HX-SKUs) can support DDR5 64009 up to 192 GB for a responsive experience. Integrated Thunderbolt™ 4 and discrete Thunderbolt™ 5 support opens up the possibilities for Intel® Core™ Ultra processor (HX-SKU) laptops to connect multiple displays and expand storage options. Also, they come with advanced wireless technologies like Wi-Fi 78 and Bluetooth® 5.4 to stay connected without cables.

 

Intel® Core™ Ultra Processors (H & U-SKUs)

Next-Gen Performance and Ready for the AI Era

  • For premium and thin & light laptop users, Intel® Core™ Ultra processors (H & U-SKUs) offer 3D performance hybrid architecture with P-cores, E-cores, and low power E-cores. Both processors also feature built-in graphics for more options.
  • Intel® Core™ Ultra processors (H-SKUs) have a built-in Intel® Arc™ GPU1 with up to eight Xe-cores for advanced visual technologies like XMX AI engine support and XeSS upscaling in games.
  • Intel® Core™ Ultra processors (U-SKUs) are available with Intel® graphics support with four Xe-cores for stunning visuals while maintaining power efficiency.
  • Both processors contain Intel’s signature NPU accelerator for handling AI assistants, generative AI, and more.

Elevated Performance that Lasts All Day

  • Unparalleled efficiency with Intel’s power-saving technologies. Intel® Core™ Ultra processors (H & U-SKUs) are ready for working, learning, and gaming all day.
  • Intel® Core™ Ultra processors (H-SKUs) feature Endurance Gaming Mode for optimized performance when gaming on battery. They also house Intel® Dynamic Tuning Technology to intelligently tune performance and adapt power draw based on use and temperature.
  • Both SKUs offer the new Memory Power Management feature and Power Share 3.0 with other graphics.

Uncompromised Connectivity
Intel® Core™ Ultra processors (H & U-SKUs) are ready to be the center of workflows with plenty of connectivity options. Get up to four 4K displays with HDMI 2.1 and DisplayPort 2.1 as well as Intel® Intelligent Display.2 These processors also feature Thunderbolt™ 4 technology for more expansion. Intel® Core™ Ultra processors (H-SKUs) additionally offer discrete Thunderbolt™ 5 support for more bandwidth.

Both processors are also ready to connect wirelessly with Wi-Fi 78 and Bluetooth® 5.4 support.
