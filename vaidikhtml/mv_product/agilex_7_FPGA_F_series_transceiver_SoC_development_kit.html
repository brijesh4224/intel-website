Agilex™ 7 FPGA F-Series Transceiver-SoC Development Kit (P-Tile and E-Tile)

The Agilex™ 7 FPGA F-Series Transceiver-SoC development kit offers a complete design environment that includes hardware and software for developing Agilex™ 7 FPGA F-Series designs. This kit is recommended for developing custom Arm processor-based SoC designs and evaluating transceiver performance.​

Learn more about Agilex™ 7 FPGA F-Series devices ›​

Use this Agilex™ 7 FPGA F-Series FPGA Transceiver-SoC Development Kit to:​

  • Evaluate SoC designs with the hard processor system (HPS).
  • Evaluate transceiver performance up to 58 Gbps for E-Tile.​
  • Generate and check pseudo-random binary sequence (PRBS) patterns.
  • Dynamically change differential output voltage (VOD) pre-emphasis and equalization settings to optimize transceiver performance for your channel.
  • Perform jitter analysis.
