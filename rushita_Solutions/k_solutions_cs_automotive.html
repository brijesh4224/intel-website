Intel Arc Graphics for Automotive

Our automotive discrete graphics processing unit (dGPU) addresses the growing demand for compute performance in increasingly sophisticated vehicle cockpits. By adding the Intel® Arc™ graphics for Automotive to our existing portfolio of AI-enhanced SDV system-on-chips, we offer automakers an open, flexible and scalable platform solution that brings next level, high fidelity experiences to the vehicle.

Automakers can now design a single vehicle platform that scales across trim levels. Entry and mid-level models can leverage the Intel SDV SoC, while higher-end vehicles can benefit from the added horsepower of the dGPU for even more premium features. This approach streamlines development as software is fully compatible across the integrated GPU and the discrete GPU.

  • dGPU Fact Sheet: The new discrete GPU for the automotive industry is set to elevate in-vehicle AI capabilities in 2025.
