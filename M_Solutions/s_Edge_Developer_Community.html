Innovator of the Month

Rupesh Sreeraman

Perseverance is the key to success.

Rupesh Sreeraman, originally from Kollam, Kerala, India, is a technical architect at Trenser Technology Solutions. Rupesh's exceptional efforts leave a lasting impression that transcends his specific project work. He has created FastSD CPU—the "fast" stable diffusion running on the CPU. This power-packed project has more than 1,000 GitHub stars. He has also authored over 100 articles about OpenVINO™ toolkit, AI PC, computer vision, generative AI, and various other technologies on his technology blog, Nolowiz.
