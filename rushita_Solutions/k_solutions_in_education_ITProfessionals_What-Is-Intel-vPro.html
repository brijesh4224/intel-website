Boost PC Protection with Multilayer, Hardware-Based Security

The security threat landscape is constantly changing. Additionally, the growth of the hybrid and remote workforce has introduced new complexities and potential attack surface vulnerabilities, making it more difficult for IT to protect PCs outside an organization’s firewall.

Intel vPro® is built to address these advanced security challenges with integrated, multilayer security features designed for dispersed fleets. Available exclusively on Intel vPro®-based PCs, Intel® Hardware Shield adds out-of-the-box protections above and below the OS to help reduce the device’s attack surface, including AI-powered threat detection that actively monitors against attacks. Security integrated at the hardware level helps prevent malware injection, protect user log-in credentials, and keep applications and sensitive data secure; below-the-OS features help defend against firmware-based attacks. Additionally, PCs built on Intel vPro® Enterprise for Windows can be reached even if the OS is down, allowing your team to access devices that have been compromised regardless of where the device is located.8 9

With the multilayer security protections of the Intel vPro® platform available, you and your team can gain peace of mind knowing that intelligent security features are actively helping to protect users from malicious attacks and your business from breaches, all without bogging down productivity for the user or IT.

Get Complete Remote Management

With hybrid work now being used as a strategic workforce policy, offices and employee devices are now anywhere employees choose to work. This can make it more costly and time-consuming for IT teams to monitor, manage, and protect PCs.

To help IT simplify and streamline support in order to provide an improved end-user experience, the Intel vPro® platform provides modern hardware-based management capabilities via Intel® Active Management Technology (Intel® AMT). Intel® AMT, available on all devices built on Intel vPro® Enterprise for Windows, provides the advanced remote management tools IT teams need to manage a distributed fleet, enabling the ability to:

  • Remotely repair corrupted drivers, application software, or even the OS of nonresponsive systems.
  • Manage inventory better by detecting and monitoring the status of all endpoints on a network, independent of their power state, OS status, or connectivity type.
  • Keep the whole fleet consistent and up to date with remote, scheduled automated software patches and updates.
  • Reduce the number of software-based interruptions users experience by remotely waking systems and patching them during off hours.

Additionally, Intel vPro® remote management capabilities can help positively impact the sustainability efforts at your business while helping you stretch your budget and increase IT efficiency. For example, IT can perform PC maintenance or resolve issues without a truck roll or in-person desk visit, helping to lower costs and improve IT productivity. To help control energy costs and increase efficiency, IT can remotely set device wake times or power down entire fleets when they’re not needed. Improve PC Reliability and Stability

Stable, validated hardware helps to keep your business running and your users productive and can enable smoother fleet management and business continuity, allowing you to focus your IT resources on business growth and innovation.

The Intel vPro® platform delivers the latest PC platform technologies in one integrated and validated solution. We partner with OEMs and OS providers to conduct thousands of tests and feedback loops each year to certify that Intel vPro® platform-based PCs meet rigorous standards and are validated for business. This also means that Intel maintains corporate platform validation for multiple OS versions at a time, so IT can transition from one OS generation to the next on their own schedule. Our multistep validation process also helps ensure that the highest possible standards are maintained throughout the PC value chain, from Intel to OEM to IT to end user. This gives IT and end users the stability and reliability of a true business-class device.

A key component to the stability and reliability offered with Intel vPro® Enterprise for Windows PCs is the Intel® Stable IT Platform Program (Intel® SIPP) Intel® SIPP aims to keep hardware components consistent for 15 months from product launch, including the silicon on the motherboard, the design, and each physical component. IT can be confident in scaling out their organization, knowing they won’t have to worry about revalidation when supplying systems around the globe.
