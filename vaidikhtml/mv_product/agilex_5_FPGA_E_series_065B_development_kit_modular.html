Agilex™ 5 FPGA E-Series 065B Modular Development Kit

Agilex™ 5 FPGA E-Series 065B Modular Development Kit delivers a complete prototyping and reference platform design environment that includes both hardware and software. It is a general-purpose evaluation board in PCIe form factor with HPS hardware features enabling you to develop industrial, video, and other embedded and edge applications.​

Learn more about Agilex™ 5 FPGA E-Series devices ›

The development kit is comprised of:​

  • A modular board with its own set up features and connectors allowing it to function standalone.
  • A feature-rich carrier card that expands its capabilities, making it suitable for various embedded designs and edge applications – from video and vision processing to factory automation, healthcare imaging systems, and many more.​
