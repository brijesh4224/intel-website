The Intel® FPGA Academic Program offers free teaching and research resources exclusively for faculty and staff.

Apply Now

What You Get

  • Request donations of design software licenses and hardware
  • Request solutions and source material for FPGA-based lab exercises
  • Get priority access to Intel® Developer Cloud for FPGA for academics

Who Is Eligible

  • Faculty and staff members from any university, college, or not-for-profit research institute.

How to Apply

  • Create a MyIntel account (if you do not already have one) using your academic email address.
  • Complete the membership request form.
