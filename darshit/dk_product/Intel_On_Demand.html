Intel on demand services model

What Is Intel On Demand?

Intel On Demand is a service primarily managed by Intel® and hardware providers to enable select CPU-based features and capabilities. It is offered via two models: one-time license-based feature activation and metering-based consumption.

Activation Model

Adapt quickly to changing requirements and optimize for workloads using the activation model. It is a one-time license-based feature-activation during manufacturing or as a post-purchase upgrade.

Intel On Demand capable SKUs on 4th and 5th Gen Intel® Xeon® Scalable processors can be found on ark.intel.com including available upgrades. Learn about Intel® Accelerator Engines and Intel® VROC here.
