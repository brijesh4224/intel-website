“Technology will play a critical role in building a digital future that is equitable, accessible, and inclusive for all.”

- Pat Gelsinger, Intel CEO

Intel’s corporate purpose is to create world-changing technology that improves the life of every person on the planet. Intel put the silicon in Silicon Valley, and today we are applying our reach, scale, and resources to deliver on bold goals. Technology is becoming increasingly central to every aspect of human existence, and semiconductors are its foundation. The digitization of everything was markedly accelerated by COVID-19 and has spurred innovation and new models of learning, work, business, and care. Disruptive Emerging Technologies such as Ubiquitous Computing, Pervasive Connectivity, AI, and Cloud to Edge Infrastructure are driving rapid digital transformation.

As Intel looks ahead to the next decade, our ambitions have never been greater to unleash the power of data and advance next-generation innovations to drive a positive impact on business, society, and the planet. Thus, Intel launched its new RISE 2030 strategies to create a more responsible, inclusive, and sustainable future, enabled through its technology and the expertise and passion of its employees. Intel's goal is to make technology fully inclusive and expand digital readiness for all. Intel is committed to collaborate with 30 countries governments, enabling digital access to 30 thousand institutions, and train 30 million people for current and future jobs.
