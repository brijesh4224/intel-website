Why Intel®: Powerful Platforms Help Drive Mission Success

Federal and aerospace customers needing edge compute are challenged by proprietary, custom-built, high-cost solutions that offer little reusability or margin for growth. These limitations make it harder to innovate and add new capabilities while also meeting strict requirements for certification, ruggedization, and performance in a small footprint. As an industry leader with a broad portfolio, Intel® helps enterprises and agencies better understand how to integrate COTS solutions for successful edge-to-cloud use cases.
