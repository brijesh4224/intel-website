What is the Intel® Developer Catalog?

This catalog is a consolidated resource for key Intel® software products that support open ecosystems and an end-to-end portfolio of technology across multiple use cases, including AI, client, cloud, 5G and edge, and gaming. This curated experience makes it easier for developers to access reference designs, toolkits, and ready-to-launch containers for faster time to market and helps preserve interoperability through upgrades.
