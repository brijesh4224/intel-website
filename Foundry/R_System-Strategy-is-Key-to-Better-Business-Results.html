The Linchpin to Better Business Results with AI is a System Strategy

Making AI accessible across cloud, network, PC and edge requires a fundamental shift in the industry’s approach.

News

June 4,2024

Contact Intel PR
Follow Intel Newsroom on social:

Businesses demand value from their technology investments, particularly AI. While advanced foundation models garner a lot of attention in the press, enterprises are more focused on realizing business outcomes as quickly and easily as possible.

As a systems architecture company, Intel's software business includes everything from development tools and security software to different platforms for various industries. Jeff McVeigh, Intel corporate vice president and general manager of the Software Engineering Group, explains Intel’s open approach to simplify AI deployment and improve time-to-value.

There are opportunities in embracing AI and the cloud – even for businesses without the right combination of infrastructure or expertise currently in place. That’s where the Intel® Tiber™ portfolio of business solutions plays a key role.
