Creating Positive Impact Through Corporate Responsibility

At Intel, our commitment to corporate responsibility and sustainability leadership is deeply integrated throughout our business. Not only do we strive to create an inclusive and positive work environment where every employee feels they have a voice and a sense of belonging, but we are also proactive in our efforts to reduce our environmental footprint through efficient and responsible use of natural resources and materials.

We seek to leverage our leadership position in the global tech ecosystem to help our company and the industry make greater strides in corporate responsibility: environmental sustainability, social impact, and governance. Through our “RISE” strategy and goals, we aim to leverage our leadership position in the global technology ecosystem to create a more responsible, inclusive and sustainable world, enabled through our technology and the expertise and passion of employees.

Environmental Sustainability

We believe that our technology plays a vital role in addressing the planet’s sustainability challenges. We actively pursue new ways to reduce emissions and improve energy management through conservation, renewable energy, efficient building design, and other efforts. This includes our commitment to net-zero greenhouse gas emissions across our global operations by 2040.

Diversity and Inclusion

Cultivating a diverse and inclusive workforce is instrumental in driving innovation and supporting business growth. We have a comprehensive strategy that includes implementing partnerships and programs to increase retention and advancement of women and underrepresented populations within our workplace, as well as several diversity initiatives that involve partnering with diverse suppliers and diversifying our venture portfolio.

Social Impact

We are committed to creating a better world through the power of our technology and the passion of our employees. We provide many high-skill, high-paying jobs around the world that impact various local economies. We believe that supporting and collaborating with the local communities where we operate broadens access to opportunity and helps to inspire the next generation of innovators.

Supply Chain Responsibility

Actively managing and improving performance across our supply chain reduces risks, improves product quality, and helps us achieve environmental and social goals. This creates value for our company and our customers. In order to ensure that our supply chain is resilient, responsible, and respectful of human rights, we have many robust programs in place to educate and engage the suppliers that support our global manufacturing operations.
