Agilex™ 7 FPGA and SoC FPGA F-Series

F-Series devices are general-purpose FPGAs built on Intel 10 nm SuperFin process technology. They are ideal for a wide range of applications across many markets, with features including transceiver rates up to 58 Gbps, advanced digital signal processing (DSP) blocks supporting multiple precisions of fixed-point and floating-point operations, and high-performance crypto blocks.​

Benefits

Core Architecture Delivers Design Optimization Advantages

Second-generation Intel® Hyperflex™ FPGA Architecture offers key advantages to enable significant design optimization and deliver higher performance, lower total power, greater design functionality, and increased designer productivity.

Accelerate Digital Signal Processing (DSP) and Boost Performance

First FPGA to support hardened half-precision floating point (FP16) and BFLOAT16, providing up to 38 tera floating point operations per second (TFLOPS) of DSP performance (FP16) for higher performance/watt in artificial intelligence and other compute-intensive applications.

Maintain Confidentiality and Integrity with Robust Security Features

Dedicated Secure Device Manager (SDM) controls configuration, authentication, bitstream encryption, key protection, tamper sensors, and active tamper detection and response, allowing you to select those features necessary to meet your security objectives.

Key Features

Second-Generation Intel® Hyperflex™ FPGA Architecture

Intel® Hyperflex™ FPGA architecture adds bypassable registers called Hyper-Registers distributed throughout the FPGA fabric and are available on every interconnect routing segment and at the inputs of all functional blocks.

Variable-Precision DSP

The innovative DSP architecture, allows configuration of the DSP blocks to support variable-precision signal processing, floating point and integar addition, multiplication, multiply-add, and multiply-accumulate.

DDR4 Interface

Hardened memory controllers offer performance, density, low power, and control, solving memory system bottlenecks in high-performance computing and the data center.

Quad-core Arm Cortex-A53 SoC

Integrated hardened quad-core Arm Cortex-A53 processor option.
