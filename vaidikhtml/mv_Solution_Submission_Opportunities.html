Requirements Definitions

Intel® Partner Solutions

An aggregation of products—optimized on Intel® architecture—tailored to solve industry or use-case specific business challenges.

Intel® IoT RFP Ready Kits

Use-case focused, enterprise-grade technology bundle comprised of hardware and software that allows for a high level of customization and intended for System Integrators or Enterprise Developers to accelerate development. Learn more.

Intel® IoT Market Ready Solutions

End-to-end Intel®-enabled solutions that accelerate the adoption of IoT by vetting offers for completeness, repeatability, and scalability. Learn more.

Intel Inside® Program

Document all included Intel® technologies and forecast quantities for each that will be sold over a 12-month period to qualify.

Commercially Deployed

Verify if the solution has been purchased and deployed in a customer or client setting.

Optimized Design

The solution design is tuned and tested for optimized performance on a particular workload, application, or use case.

Benchmarked Performance

The solution has been tested to show technical value compared to an alternative offering using either an industry standard benchmark or an application or use case specific benchmark.

Sales & Marketing Collateral

Website and marketing collateral that covers the included hardware and software as well as the value and benefits to the end user in target vertical segments.

Procurement Process

Describe how an end user would purchase a solution from a single source.

Customer Success Story

Verifiable customer documentation that describes a business challenge and how the solution was successful in driving a positive business outcome.
