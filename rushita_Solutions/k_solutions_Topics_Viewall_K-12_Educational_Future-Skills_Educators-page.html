From Empathy to Innovation

Combining technical and social emotional learning enables students to recognize and understand the people they’re creating for by building their empathy and communication skills—what we call “essential skills.” Students use these experiences to uncover opportunities and prototype solutions faster, because students who learn to explore and fail with confidence are more creative and resilient.
