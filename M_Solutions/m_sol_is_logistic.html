Gain Supply Chain Visibility Using Azure IoT Central

Microsoft Surface and Xbox supply chain teams look to achieve real-time visibility into shipment locations. By utilizing solutions like Intel® Connected Logistics Platform (Intel® CLP) they are able achieve end-to-end, track-and-trace capabilities to gain precise location data as well as ambient conditions like shock, light, and temperature. With this improved insight, the Microsoft team could monitor the shipments, down to the package level, in almost real time allowing them to address any tampering or delays in a timely fashion.
