Thunderbolt™ Technology: Revolutionary Connectivity
Thunderbolt™ technology is more than a USB-C connector. Although it uses the same oval shape connector as USB-C, Thunderbolt™ technology certification mandates higher minimum performance and capabilities for cables, PCs, and accessories. You benefit from the best-in-class product capabilities to deliver the fastest cable solution for connecting to power and to all your docked accessories and monitors.
