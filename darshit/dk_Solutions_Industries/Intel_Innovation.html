Industry Conversations

 

 

Industry Conversation with Priyanka Sharma

Don't miss this insightful interview! In this engaging discussion, we delve into the world of cloud-native technologies, AI, and the future of development. Priyanka shares her journey from startup founder to leading the CNCF, discusses the challenges and opportunities ahead, and offers valuable insights for developers.

Listen in as we will talk about the evolution of cloud-native computing and the growing importance of AI, the role of open source and the challenges and opportunities of integrating AI workloads into cloud-native environments.

Industry Conversation with Solomon Hykes

The conversation between Arun Gupta and Solomon Hykes delves into the evolution of container technology and its impact on modern software development. Hear about the origins of Docker and its role in revolutionizing application deployment. Listen to the challenges faced by developers in managing complex software pipelines and the emergence of Dagger as a solution.

Hear how the importance of a unified platform for managing software pipelines and the benefits of a modular, cross-language approach are key for developers. The conversation touches on the role of security and the integration of security tools within the software development pipeline. Listen in for these and more during this Selects conversation.
