On Oct. 10, 2024, Intel launched its Intel® Core™ Ultra 200S desktop processors (codenamed Arrow Lake-S), ushering in the first enthusiast desktop AI PCs. The new processors are the complete enthusiast solution, offering excellent performance in AI and content creation, powering an immersive gaming experience, and delivering landmark power reductions across everyday applications, gaming, and creation applications.

The new processors join the Intel Core Ultra 200V mobile processors (codenamed Lunar Lake) in bringing unprecedented scale to the AI PC. Through partnerships with leading independent software vendors, broad ecosystem support and more than 500 optimized AI models, PCs powered by the newest Intel Core Ultra processors empower users to take full advantage of what AI can offer.

Intel Core Ultra 200S desktop processors will power the industry’s most complete and capable desktop AI PCs from the world’s top OEM partners starting October 24.
