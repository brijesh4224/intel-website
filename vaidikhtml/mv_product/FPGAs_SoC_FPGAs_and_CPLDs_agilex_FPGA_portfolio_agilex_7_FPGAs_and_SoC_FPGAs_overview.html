Agilex™ 7 FPGA and SoC FPGA

The highest-performance FPGAs deliver industry-leading fabric and IO speeds, ideal for most bandwidth, compute, and memory-intensive applications.

Agilex™ 7 devices deliver ~2X better fabric performance per watt1 compared to competing 7 nm FPGAs. They also offer integrated Arm-based processors, transceivers up to 116Gbps, PCI Express (PCIe) 5.0, and Compute Express Link (CXL), and 32GB HBM2e. These features make them ideal for a wide range of applications in many markets including data center, networking, broadcast, defense, and industrial.

Agilex™ 7 FPGAs and SoC FPGAs F-Series

Targeted for a wide range of applications, this series is built using Intel 10 nm SuperFin process technology, and offers transceiver rates up to 58 Gbps, advanced DSP blocks supporting multiple precisions of fixed-point and floating-point operations, and high-performance crypto blocks.

Agilex™ 7 FPGAs and SoC FPGAs I-Series

Targeted for bandwidth-intensive applications, this series is built using Intel 10 nm SuperFin process technology and expands upon F-Series device features, offering transceiver rates up to 116 Gbps, PCIe 5.0 support, and cache- and memory-coherent attach to processors with Compute Express Link (CXL).

Agilex™ 7 FPGAs and SoC FPGAs M-Series

Targeted for compute and memory-intensive applications, this series is built using Intel 7 process technology and expands upon I-Series device features, offering in-package high-bandwidth memory (HBM), memory interfaces for DDR5 SDRAM, and a hard memory Network-on-Chip (NoC) to maximize memory bandwidth.
