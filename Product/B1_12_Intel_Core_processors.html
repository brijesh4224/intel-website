Intel® Core™ Processors Product Brief

Power up your edge devices with an LGA-socket CPU that delivers higher performance, up to 24 cores, 32 threads, single-core turbo frequencies up to 5.8 GHz, more cache, and expansive I/O. Performance hybrid architecture, PCIe 5.0 connectivity, and DDR5-5600 memory enable flexibility for the latest AI use cases, with intelligent performance that adapts based on available thermal and power headroom.
