Chiplet Architecture Accelerates Delivery of Industry-Leading FPGA Features and Capabilities

Using chiplets to add complex functions to semiconductors enables faster time-to-market for leading-edge capabilities, enabling Intel FPGA customers to innovate with the latest I/O, interface, and other functions as soon as possible.​

Heterogeneous Integration Innovations via Chiplets: Empowering You to Innovate Faster

Intel’s approach allows the integration of chiplets regardless of their process node, foundry, and IP developer—providing maximum flexibility to deliver the needed functionality. Using this method, Intel has delivered several innovations to the FPGA industry, including PCIe 5.0 x16 support at the full 32GT/s data rate, 116G transceivers, and high sample rate data converters.
