Intel Code of Conduct: How We Win

To achieve our goals, we must execute with discipline, operate with urgency, and act with transparency, honesty, and integrity in every aspect of our business. The Intel Code of Conduct sets forth how we work and win with integrity each day. – Pat Gelsinger

Integrity in Everything We Do

Intel’s long-standing commitment to doing business ethically and in compliance with the letter and spirit of the law is reinforced in a new Intel Code of Conduct that increases its emphasis on our core values as essential guidelines.

 

We believe in doing the right things right—everywhere we do business.
