Developer Resources for AI & HPC

AI & HPC Ecosystem Developer Resources

Intel collaborates with key members of the AI and high-performance computing (HPC) ecosystem to enable developers to get the most performance from their software, models, and libraries running on Intel® hardware. Explore resources and learn from Intel customers how to get the most value from Intel CPUs, GPUs, and AI accelerators.

Hugging Face*

Hugging Face* and Intel collaborate to build state-of-the-art hardware and software AI acceleration with transformer models. Intel's AI Tools, Intel® Neural Compressor, Intel® Distribution of OpenVINO™ toolkit, and SigOpt* accelerate the Hugging Face Optimum library.

Explore

PyTorch* Foundation

Intel is honored to join the PyTorch* Foundation as a premier member. Intel's contributions to PyTorch started in 2018 with the vision to democratize access to AI through ubiquitous hardware and open software.

Explore

Red Hat*

Collaborations between Red Hat* and Intel take advantage of AI Tools, OpenVINO toolkit, and the cnvrg.io* platform available in the Red Hat OpenShift* AI managed cloud service (a joint AI developer program).

Explore

Microsoft*

Microsoft* and Intel use Intel's latest hardware and optimized AI software for advanced data and AI capabilities with Microsoft .NET, Microsoft Azure*, and Microsoft Nuance*. This results in significant improvements and potential cost efficiencies within developer communities.

Explore

Argonne National Laboratory

Intel, Hewlett Packard Enterprise*, and Argonne National Laboratory collaborated to build Aurora, one of the fastest supercomputers on the planet, for AI and HPC researchers.
