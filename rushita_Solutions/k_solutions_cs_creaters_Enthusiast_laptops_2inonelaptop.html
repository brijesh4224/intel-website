/2 in 1 Laptops

A Single Device Built for Versatility

2 in 1 laptops powered by the latest Intel® technologies deliver the performance and mobility that you expect from a laptop. Then get the flexibility to switch to a tablet with a touchscreen whenever you want.

2 in 1 laptops switch modes—tablet, tent, or presentation—for the way you work, learn, and play. These sleek, ultraportable devices provide powerful performance and long battery life, so they’re ready when you are. You can create a spreadsheet in laptop mode, collaborate on a report in tent mode, or watch videos in tablet mode while relaxing on the couch.

Benefits of 2 in 1 Laptops

The best 2 in 1 laptops let you reimagine creation and entertainment by combining high performance, immersive graphics, and multiple modes. Finding the right 2 in 1 starts with the processor. 2 in 1 laptops featuring Intel® Core™ processors deliver performance powerful enough for everything from entertainment to gaming to keeping up with social media.

You’ll want enough memory, or RAM, to run your favorite applications. If your expectations include gaming and streaming on the device, select models with higher-performance storage, graphics, and display.

To write or draw, choose a stylus with a nib and grip that will let you comfortably take notes or create art. Also consider how the touchscreen technology impacts image clarity, scratch resistance, and whether you can activate it with bare hands, gloves, or other devices.U.

With performance and features improving all the time, you can use a 2 in 1 laptop for all your needs, enjoy the latest features, and take it everywhere you go.
