FDL Tackles Deep Space Exploration

Frontier Development Lab uses Intel® AI technologies powered by oneAPI to help solve challenges in deep space exploration.

At a glance

  • Frontier Development Lab (FDL) is a public private partnership between NASA, the SETI Institute, Trillium Technologies, and commercial AI partners.
  • Intel® Xeon® processors and a software portfolio of tools, libraries, and framework optimizations for end-to-end AI workflows powered by Intel® oneAPI tools, are helping FDL solve the challenges associated with deep space exploration. Recent innovations such as Open Federated Learning and Intel® Software Guard Extensions, are further helping FDL understand how space radiation will affect astronauts, particularly on deep space exploration.
