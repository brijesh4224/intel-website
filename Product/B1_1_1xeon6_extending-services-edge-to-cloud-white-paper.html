White Paper: Extending Edge-to-Cloud Service

Data center, cloud, and edge architectures have evolved over decades, creating unique environments, each with its own development and deployment parameters. Today’s edge-to-cloud workflows demand that these environments integrate seamlessly. This white paper addresses a broad set of topics in edge-to-cloud deployments—including cloud, 5G, edge, AI, and cybersecurity—and proposes an integrated, edge-to-cloud architectures built on open standard hardware, software, and technologies.
