Equipment Providers

Original design manufacturers (ODM) and original equipment manufacturers (OEM) offer systems, cameras, and solutions for CPUs, GPUs, FPGAs, and VPUs built on Intel® architecture.

Software Providers

System Integrators

These providers help solve their customers’ market problems by integrating, deploying, or servicing hardware, software, and services.

Solution Aggregators or Distributors

These partners source discrete components or bundled solutions, provide training on Intel® Vision Products and ecosystem offerings, and distribute the Intel® IoT RFP Ready Kit and Intel® IoT Market Ready Solution.
