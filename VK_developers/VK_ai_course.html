AI Concepts

Computer Vision

Learn what computer vision is and how it works.

Generative AI

This subset of AI focuses on creating new, original content, and involves training and deploying AI models to generate data.

AI Inference

Inference is the process by which AI infers information from data.

Machine Learning

This technique is a subset of AI that uses models, or algorithms, to analyze large amounts of complex data.

Transfer Learning

This machine learning technique reuses a previously trained model on a new related model.
