Frequently Asked Questions

All partners can subscribe to insight.tech. However, only members of the Intel Partner Alliance IoT Community may participate in article idea submission, content sponsorship, and leads generation.

Use your email address to subscribe to our global publication, insight.tech.

As an Intel® Partner Alliance IoT Community member, you can showcase your expertise on insight.tech in the following ways

  • Propose an article idea

    • Contribute your ideas to insight.tech, the alliance’s flagship online publication. If accepted, our team will write and promote the article, helping you develop thought leadership and drive leads—all at no cost to you.

  • Sponsor content

    • Get a guaranteed spot in insight.tech with sponsored content. Advertising options ranging from white papers to podcasts will reach a highly targeted audience of more than 150,000 subscribers, with additional amplification on social media and partner channels.

  • Grow your leads

    • Insight.Tech incorporates the latest in marketing automation tools and is backed by a global promotional program. In 2019, insight.tech generated more than 40,000 leads for participants. You can manage your leads in the Intel® Lead Portal.
