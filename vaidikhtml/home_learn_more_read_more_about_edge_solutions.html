Streamline Management and Overcome Barriers

Even in low-power environments with connectivity challenges, Intel® Tiber™ Edge Platform supports edge solutions that demand low latency and high security. Key Use Cases Enabled by Intel® Tiber™ Edge Platform

Networking

Privacy, latency, bandwidth—advanced edge solutions like private 5G can solve the biggest networking challenges. Edge network nodes are highly distributed, often in challenging locations, making them difficult to manage and orchestrate. Intel® Tiber™ Edge Platform, which incorporates FlexRAN™ Reference Architecture, streamlines device management, enabling cloud-like scalability and control.

Manufacturing Quality Control

Close down data silos and deepen your control over manufacturing processes. Our growing edge ecosystem offers commercial-ready defect detection solutions built on Intel® Tiber™ Edge Platform. This means that the applications controlling your robots, sensors, and industrial PCs will be compatible with your existing enterprise infrastructure and manageable with a single pane of glass.

Retail Automation

Retail chains can improve shopping experiences and reduce inventory problems with solutions like touchless checkout and smart inventory tracking. Using the platform, retailers can update or onboard thousands of edge devices with the push of a button—or even automatically.
