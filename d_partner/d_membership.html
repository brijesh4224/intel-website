Program Information FAQs

Intel® Partner Alliance is free to join.

Complete the Intel Partner Alliance registration and online profile. We will verify your business model and compliance with our program roles. Once we accept your application, we will update your profile to reflect your membership.

  • Gold partners enjoy access to enhanced support, training, channel events, sales tools, rewards, and recognition with a Gold-tier badge.
  • Titanium partners receive advanced support, marketing resources, exclusive event invitations, recognition of expertise, and the Titanium-tier badge.

Revenue is a measurement of activities that correspond with the transactions of Intel® product sales. Intel utilizes multiple methods to measure revenue that can differ based on the role the partner plays in the Intel Partner Alliance.

  • For partner roles that involve invoices for Intel products, revenue is calculated on the Intel components purchased by a partner from an Intel Authorized Distributor/ Intel Approved Supplier.
  • Where the partner purchases an OEM branded system from an Intel Authorized Distributor/ Intel Approved Supplier, the Intel eligible content within that system will be assigned the revenue (not the cost of the entire OEM branded system).
  • Where the partner purchases an ODM product from an Approved Intel ODM, the Intel eligible content within that system will be assigned the revenue (not the cost of the entire ODM system).
  • For partners who influence Intel revenue given business models such as consulting, software and services, sales representatives and customer support can provide tools for capturing revenue.

The Recommended Customer Price listed on https://ark.intel.com/content/www/us/en/ark.html provides a guide to approximate the revenue ascribed to associated Intel products.

Roles and Tiers FAQs

Intel Partner Alliance has three tiers: Member, Gold, and Titanium.

Intel Partner Alliance utilizes a foundational set of requirements such as revenue, training, and solutions postings to determine your tier. There may be additional requirements beyond these depending on role.Read about tier requirements on our About Membership page.

Your business can unlock access to additional membership benefits based on qualifying purchases from Intel Authorized Distributors and Intel Approved Suppliers. Qualified product purchases include:

  • Processors: Any Intel® desktop, mobile, server, or workstation processor
  • Motherboards: Any Intel® server motherboard
  • Intel® Solid-State Drives (SSDs)
  • Components: Any Intel® server components (RAID controller, chassis adapter, or server system)
