Intel® Virtual RAID on CPU (Intel® VROC)

Modern all-NVMe storage systems demand a new architecture. Intel® Virtual RAID on CPU (Intel® VROC), integrated on Intel® Xeon® Processors, replaces external RAID HBAs, blending hardware RAID's robustness with software RAID's flexibility and upgradeability.

Learn more about Intel® VROC

Intel® Volume Management Device (Intel® VMD)

Intel® VMD, a feature of Intel® Xeon® Scalable processors, allows direct management of NVMe SSDs via PCIe, eliminating extra hardware. This enhances NVMe SSD performance, minimizes downtime, and adds enterprise-grade reliability and serviceability, enabling confident deployment of next-gen storage.
