Computer Vision AI by Industry

The impact of computer vision is widespread, allowing businesses across many industries to streamline operations and uncover new answers.

Global competition has made it critical to eliminate inefficiencies in manufacturing, and computer vision AI is a big part of these initiatives. With AI, manufacturers can improve quality and innovation and better manage their inventory and supply chain. AI works in conjunction with robotic and other manufacturing systems to improve system learning and system automation.

Knowing the customer is critical for the success of modern retailers, and computer vision AI provides new tools for customer service and intelligence. By utilizing this data, retailers can improve the customer experience in a meaningful way. AI can also help to cut costs through improved inventory management.

Computer vision AI can provide around-the-clock monitoring of security video feeds, providing anomaly detection that enables a higher level of protection. AI can process image input quickly and can do so for a large number of camera feeds simultaneously. Computer vision AI makes security professionals more efficient at responding to incidents. The use of AI makes video security a proactive tool that improves safety day and night.

Life sciences research is a big data undertaking and AI has the potential to deliver fresh insights from this data in record time when compared to manual analysis. AI can benefit the complete research cycle from start to finish, including basic research, molecule research, clinical trials, drug manufacturing, supply chain, and ongoing product intelligence.

The healthcare industry is increasingly leveraging AI for more precise and efficient patient interactions that can help boost the impact of diagnoses and treatment plans. With AI, healthcare practitioners get new insights into the success of diagnostics and care processes with the possibility to intervene in new and novel ways for better health outcomes.

In the face of environmental changes, farmers must produce more food to feed a growing population with less impact on the land. AI-powered precision farming provides actionable data that helps farmers grow more food while using less water, fertilizer and other resources. AI tracks precise data about moisture and temperature with computer vision AI providing critical insight into visual data such as weather, soil and other factors that can improve crop yields.
