Customer Service

Please take a moment to familiarize yourself with how to use our website by reading below, and including how to manage your orders and quotes

If you are experiencing any difficulties, please contact DesignInTools@intel.com

Using this website

The Design-In Tools Store allows you to place a web order with Intel for available motherboard design and validation tools on Intel platforms. These tools are available for use by Intel's design partners in engineering labs on proof-of-concept systems only.

For the best experience, please view this website on Google Chrome. Microsoft Edge and Mozilla Firefox on Microsoft Windows or OSX are also supported.

If you are experiencing issues with Design In Tools, please try the following prior to contacting Support:

  1. Hard refresh your browser (Crtl + F5 for Windows or Command + Shift + R for macs)
  2. Clear your browser Cache - Browser Cache Clearing Procedures
  3. Open an Incognito window on your browser
  4. Make sure your browser is updated to the latest version
  5. Reboot your computer
  6. Check to see if any Firewall or VPN is blocking access (this may require assistance from your IT Support team)

Login Access

To login and view the full catalog of products the Intel® Design-In Tools Store. To register for an Intel® Design-In Tools Store login, please register in the following places:

  1. You must have or create an Intel Business Login on Microsoft Azure with your individual corporate email account.
  2. Apply for Privileged access . If your company does not already have a CNDA on file, then you will be required to sign and file the appropriate documents. You will be emailed with your Privileged confirmation. Completion of access can take 7-10 business days.
  3. After receiving your Privileged confirmation, go back to designintools.intel.com and log in. At this point you will have access to the Intel® Design-In Tools Store

If you are accessing proprietary content, the system will prompt you to set up a Multifactor Authentication (MFA) process

  • You will need your phone & email to complete the process
  • Going forward, you will utilize your MFA process each time you Sign In
  • FAQs can be found here.
  • If after following all previous steps, you still have questions or concerns, please email DesignInTools@intel.com

Request a Quote

  1. Add all items to your shopping cart and proceed to the check out with your completed order information.
  2. Choose the “Generate Quote” button instead of the “Place Order” button to generate the quote.
  3. You will receive a confirmation of successful submission via a confirmation screen on the store as well as via email.

Quotes are valid as indicated on the bottom of the quote or via the individual products as listed. While the quote is still valid, you may return to the store and view the quote in the "My Quotes" section of the "My Accounts" area. There you may proceed to the checkout on any still valid quotes. If you do not see a quote that you previously submitted, it has since expired and you will need to create a new quote or recreate an order and proceed to the checkout.

If you have any questions, please contact customer support at DesignInTools@intel.com.

How to use a Coupon

Customers must add any discount coupons on the "View Cart" page under the list of products, which can be navigated to from the Cart icon at the top right of any page and then clicking on the “View Cart” button.

Customers may use any Intel® Design-In Tools Store discount coupons in your shopping cart before checking out. Discount coupons are valid for one year from the date of generation. Your coupon may have been generated several months prior to you receiving it and are typically one-use. If you have any trouble using your coupon, please contact our Customer Support team.

Returns

We do our best to ensure your satisfaction with our products. However, if you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may return the unused product at your expense within 30 days of purchase for a full refund unless otherwise noted in the product description. Product must be received by Intel within 10 days after the end of the return period to obtain a full refund. To ensure proper credit, please:

  • Ship from the original recipient,
  • Include a copy of the packing list, and
  • Describe the reason for the return on the packing list.

Order Cancellations

Once the order is placed it transmits automatically to the distribution center. You will need to follow our returns procedure to receive proper credit for your order.

Credit Card Pre-Authorizations

At the time you complete the order, your credit card will be pre-authorized for the amount of purchase in US Dollars. You may be required to pay credit card processing fees on select items -- any charges will be noted on the initial order submission. This pre-authorization amount is converted to a charge when the order is shipped. You will be charged once the order has been accepted. In the event that the tool or service cannot be delivered, the credit card transaction will be refunded. In the event of a long lead pre-order, refunds may be provided as store credit.

These are the top three reasons for credit card rejection and our recommended solution:

  1. Network issue: please try submitting your order again.
  2. Address mismatch: please let your bank know to pre-approve the transaction
  3. Credit limit reached: please let your bank know to pre-approve the transaction or raise your limit

Wire Transfer and Purchase Orders

Wire Transfer orders are subject to approval and are only accepted on orders over $2500.00 US dollars. Refunds on Wire Transfer purchases are for store credit only.

You will receive wire transfer instructions once your order is accepted. If we do not receive and/or cannot identify your wire transfer within 30 days, the order may be cancelled. Please ensure that your bank correctly adds the following information to your payment: Intel DIT Order # [your order number].

Net Terms are Net Zero (0) across all products, unless specified in the product description. Net Zero means that we need to receive your payment before shipping the tool or providing the service.

You are responsible for managing your internal corporate processes for payment. We cannot directly submit invoices to Ariba, or any other system.
