Intel® Deep Link

Intel® Deep Link is a technology that boosts performance and efficiency of Intel® CPUs and GPUs across different workloads.

Hyper Encode

With Intel® VPL Deep Link Hyper Encode Feature, you can leverage Intel integrated and discrete graphics to accelerate encoding. The feature is exposed through VPL API and implemented in VPL GPU runtimes delivered with Intel graphics drivers.

Refer to Developer Guide for Intel® VPL Deep Link Hyper Encode Feature published on GitHub.
