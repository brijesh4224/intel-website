Benefits

Built for Performance and Power Efficiency

With industry-leading fabric performance-per-watt on Intel 7 technology, targeted for mid-range FPGA applications requiring high performance, lower power consumption, smaller form factors, transceiver data rates 28 Gbps, and lower logic densities (down to 100k LEs).

Flexible Device with a Reliable Supply

D-Series devices leverage Intel’s advanced manufacturing capabilities for supply resiliency with consistent best-in-class lead times and reliable delivery while offering Flexible I/O with a comprehensive set of options to support a variety of configurable I/O standards.

Accelerate with AI

Accelerate AI-based workloads with the industry’s first FPGAs integrating Enhanced DSP with AI Tensor Blocks, enabling you to optimize the AI resources in the device and create custom-sized inference IP with the FPGA industry's only single push-button flow incorporating AI frameworks.
