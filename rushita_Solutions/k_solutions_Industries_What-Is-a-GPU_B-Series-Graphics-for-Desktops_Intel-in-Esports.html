The Power and the Glory

From LANs to sold out arenas, Intel has been at the beating heart of esports for over 17 years. Powering the gaming PCs responsible for helping heros rise at home and those who compete in front of millions around the world.

Intel Esport Partnerships

Intel has partnered with the leading esports leagues, brands, and publishers for nearly two decades, delivering end-to-end pro gaming solutions built on world-class technology that bring spectacular events to the world.

Electronic Sports League

Intel is the global technology partner of the Electronic Sports League (ESL), the world’s largest esports network and pioneer of competitive gaming. Founded in 2000, ESL operates high profile, international leagues, and events — all running on Intel.

Intel Esport Partnerships

Intel has partnered with the leading esports leagues, brands, and publishers for nearly two decades, delivering end-to-end pro gaming solutions built on world-class technology that bring spectacular events to the world.

Tencent League of Legends Pro League 1

Since its founding in 2013, the LPL* has been the premier League of Legends* competition in China. Powered by Intel, the league delivers world-class esports experiences to players and fans around the world.

Intel® Extreme Masters

Started in 2006, Intel® Extreme Masters is the longest-running global esports tour with the world’s best talent.

ESL One

Powered by Intel, ESL One holds events in the world’s biggest stadiums featuring the top players and teams.

ESL CS: GO Pro League

The longest-running CS:GO league, with the best teams from Europe, North America, Asia-Pacific, and South America, is supported by Intel.

Intel® Grand Slam

The Intel® Grand Slam awards $1 million USD to the first team that wins four eligible CS:GO tournaments in a span of 10 events.
