Agilex™ 5 FPGAs and SoC FPGAs E-Series

Optimized for power and size—ideal for intelligent applications at the edge, embedded, and more. This series is manufactured using Intel 7 technology and offers transceiver rate options of 17Gbps and 28Gbps, a multi-core Arm SoC, and enhanced DSP with AI Tensor Blocks.

Agilex™ 5 FPGAs and SoC FPGAs D-Series

Optimized for performance and power efficiency—ideal for various applications across multiple markets. This series is manufactured using Intel 7 technology and offers transceiver rates up to 28Gbps, a multi-core Arm SoC, and enhanced DSP with AI Tensor Blocks.
