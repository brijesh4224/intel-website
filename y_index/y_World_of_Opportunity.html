A World of Opportunity

By volunteering with amazing organizations like the Tech Museum of Innovation, we can help create a world where everyone can reach their full potential.

Transcript

- Social impact at Intel is about creating a world where everyone can reach their full potential. At Intel, we encourage employees to volunteer in the community. My role at Intel is to help our employees volunteer in schools and nonprofit organizations to help meet the needs of hunger, education, environmental sustainability, and diversity.

Giving back is part of Intel's culture. Our employees volunteer more than a million hours a year in schools and nonprofit organizations. With the help of the Intel Foundation, we can help our employees amplify their passion in the community by matching their time at $10 an hour.

We're here at The Tech Museum of Innovation in San Jose, California. I love coming to Girls Day at the Tech, because it allows girls and their families to explore exhibits such as robotics, virtual reality, and cybersecurity.

I come to work every day to empower our employees to volunteer in the community. Volunteering is just one of the ways Intel gives back. My name is Rita Holiday, and I am Intel. Learn all about Intel's social impact at intel.com/responsibility.
