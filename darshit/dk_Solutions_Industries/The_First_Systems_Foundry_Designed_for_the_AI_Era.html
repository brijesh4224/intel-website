The Landscape of Technology is Changing

AI chips consume more power than 61% of countries.1

The projected compute growth for ML training is >3x per year over the next decade.2

Compute is outpacing memory bandwidth.3

Geographically-diverse supply of advanced semiconductors is critical to supply chain resiliency.

Customer Needs are Shifting

Foundry revenue from HPC/AI designs continues to increase.

Chiplets are on track to surpass monolithic die by 2028.4

Today’s and tomorrow’s solutions require system technology co-optimization (STCO).

The World Needs a Systems Foundry for the AI Era

Compute-intensive applications like AI and HPC demand greater performance improvements than can be delivered by the advancement of semiconductor manufacturing alone. We need new approaches to address rising power consumption, satisfy data throughput requirements, and minimize operating expenses. Foundries must optimize at the system level, from silicon to software and at every layer in between.
