Accelerating Computer Vision Model Development & Deployment with Intel® Geti™ and OpenVINO™

Organizations building computer vision systems require extensive timelines and separate solutions for AI model development and deployment. In this session, we explore leveraging the tight integration between Intel® Geti™ software and OpenVINO™ toolkit to streamline vision model training and build scalable deployment solutions. Demos will walk through the complete process of building and deploying an AI model with best-in-class inferencing on Intel hardware.

Intel Geti Software: Breaking the bottlenecks of AI development with an agile intuitive platform

Join us as Paula Ramos and Ashutosh Kumar introduce you to Intel® Geti™ software, built from the ground up to make machine learning accessible to everyone in your organization. Innovations in AI and Computer Vision (CV) contribute positively to the success of organizations around the world. However, the AI + CV development and deployment process remains prohibitive in terms of cost, time and expert knowledge in the field.
