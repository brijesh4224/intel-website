Intel Geti Software Accelerates AI Model Training for Real-Time Nerve Detection in Samsung Ultrasound Systems

Samsung Medison’s NerveTrack™ is an innovative ultrasound feature used to identify nerve structures in real time during the application of anesthesia. Training of NerveTrack™’s deep learning inference models requires thousands of annotated ultrasound reference images. The image annotation process is best performed by doctors with years of medical training and experience in identifying the small, elusive nerve structures.
