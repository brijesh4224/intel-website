Intel® Core™ Processor Family

Delivering robust, real-world performance, Intel® Core™ processors give laptop users the power they can rely on for casual gaming, multitasking, and reliable connectivity.

Performance where it counts most

Now, laptop users can experience seamless multitasking, smooth, no-lag gaming and video, and the power to get the most out of high-end creativity software.

Better multitasking

With Intel® Thread Director, the Intel Core processors dynamically allocate workload tasks between efficiency and performance cores. So users can render in the background while editing video—or game, stream and record all at once.

Intel® Graphics for gaming

Using Intel® Graphics in endurance gaming mode, users can enjoy even higher frame rates per second in full HD for longer, more immersive gameplay.

Real power for creators

Creators can bring their vision to life using today’s most demanding creativity software—with outstanding display quality and full 8K HDR support.
