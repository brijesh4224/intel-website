Deliver Performance and Connectivity Wherever Innovation Requires

Businesses and civic organizations across the world are leveraging near-real-time information from connected, intelligent devices to increase operational visibility, efficiency, and safety and to accelerate decision-making and innovation.

Making this possible first requires the Internet of Things—physical devices (such as cameras, sensors, and other everyday technologies) that collect and share data about their use and environment over a communications network, combined with powerful computing capabilities to perform analytics to turn data into insights.

Gaining near-real-time access to this information requires more capabilities at the network’s edge so that data processing, AI analysis, and analytics happen closer to data sources. This intelligent edge must also connect seamlessly with cloud resources to rapidly generate insights.

At Intel, we’re bringing together our investments in edge compute, networking, and AI to help maximize IoT results. We work alongside industry leaders to prioritize industry standards, and we continuously collaborate with our ecosystem partners to bring ready-to-deploy solutions to market that are optimized and scalable with safety and security in mind. Our mission is to improve the lives of every person on the planet through technology. Flexible, reliable, and high-performance IoT solutions are a critical part of realizing it.
