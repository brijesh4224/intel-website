EPIC iO strives to make organizations and communities safer, smarter, and more connected through our robust portfolio of connectivity and AI + IoT solutions. EPIC iO’s portfolio of AI and IoT (AIoT) solutions powered by our 4G/5G connectivity enables you to join the next generation of smart organizations by solving everyday challenges, including security, safety compliance, environmental monitoring, asset monitoring and tracking, public safety, public transportation, smart city innovation, and more. All of which can be monitored in a single pane of glass through our DeepInsights™ platform that combines and manages thousands of IoT devices and camera data for new and existing applications making this information easily accessible and digestible.
