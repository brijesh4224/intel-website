Announcing the public preview launch of general-purpose D and E family VMs built on the new 5th Gen Intel® Xeon® processors.

Experience improved CPU, storage and networking performance, delivering better price performance for a wide range of customer workloads including AI. These new VMs feature Intel® Advanced Matrix Extensions (Intel® AMX), a built-in accelerator that improves the performance of deep learning training and inference.

Learn more

These new VMs are now available in public preview. Sign up here

These VMs will offer:

  • 20% higher vCPU performance and 3x larger L3 cache than the previous generation Intel Dl/D/Ev5 VMs.
  • Up to 260k IOPS, 12.5GB/s remote storage throughput.
  • Up to 200Gbps VM network bandwidth powered by Azure Boost.
  • 46% larger local SSD capacity and >3X read IOPS.
  • 84 new VM sizes in total, ranging from 2 to 192 vCPU and up to 1.8TB of memory.
  • NVMe interface for local and remote disks for all sizes.
