Move and Scale Across Clouds on a Consistent Foundation

As organizations evolve their cloud strategy, many are using a multicloud approach to support emerging workloads, reduce costs, improve security, elevate the customer experience, and address new privacy regulations. By enabling you to seamlessly scale data and applications across multiple public or private clouds, you have the flexibility to pair each workload with the best cloud resource based on factors such as cloud service provider (CSP), price, and geographic location. With high performance compute and virtualization technology at the foundation, multicloud built on Intel® platforms can give your business the speed and flexibility you need to rapidly deliver, scale, and adapt innovative services and products.
