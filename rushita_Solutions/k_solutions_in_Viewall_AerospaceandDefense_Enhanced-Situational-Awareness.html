Introducing Intel® SceneScape

Intel® SceneScape is a software platform that reaches beyond vision-based AI to realize spatial awareness from sensor data. It transforms data from many sensors to create and provide live updates to a 4D digital twin of your physical space. Digital twins can be applied to your use cases to look at past analytics, track what is happening in the present, and make predictive decisions for the future.

Track Anything, Anywhere

Enhance and extend tracking capabilities beyond vision-based AI in any industry by applying Intel SceneScape to your use case.

  • Compatible with your existing sensors to avoid vendor lock-in
  • Monitor your space, your way, with multisensor tracking
  • Stands up to harsh environments with no central connectivity needed

Track Your Assets in Near Real Time

Intel SceneScape gives users the live tracking data needed to assess the people and objects within their spaces by creating digital twins of their physical environment.

  • Generate four-dimensional digital twins
  • Near real-time tracking data to assess what is happening while it’s happening
  • Can detect a person or an object at multiple angles yet only display the asset once

Easy to Use and Deploy

Easily deploy powerful AI applications across your business and technical teams. Using Intel SceneScape is a three-step process: upload a map of your space, calibrate your sensors, and go.

  • User-friendly for all members of your team with a low-code interface.
  • Eliminate the need to develop custom solutions from the ground up.

Digital Twins Help Connect the Digital and Real Worlds

Digital twins are virtual representations of real-world objects, systems, or environments, informed by real-world data inputs. Using digital twins, businesses can gain deeper visibility, optimize workflows to prevent bottlenecks, automate awareness, and predict future outcomes, creating more positive engagements and quality experiences.
