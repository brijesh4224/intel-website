The Intel® Infrastructure Processing Unit (Intel® IPU) Adapter E2100 delivers infrastructure acceleration, virtual storage enablement and enhanced security features.

Enable Rapid Innovation for the Modern Data Center

Featuring a rich packet-processing pipeline, 200GbE bandwidth, and including NVMe, compression and crypto accelerators. The Arm Neoverse N1 compute complex allows customer-provided software to execute features, ranging from complex packet-processing pipelines to storage transport, device management, and telemetry. By utilizing a combination of acceleration hardware and software running in the compute complex, this IPU adapter enables rapid innovation necessary for the modern data center.

  • Separation and isolation of infrastructure workloads.
  • Offload virtualized networks to the IPU where the accelerators can process tasks more efficiently.
  • Replace previously necessary local disk storage with detached virtualized storage.
