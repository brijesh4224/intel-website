Inspire Your Modern Workforce with Next-Gen PCs

A recent multinational IDC survey shows just how important providing next-gen PCs to new employees can be.

Having the Right PC Is More Critical Now Than Ever Before

Companies continue to navigate a world where many employees have transitioned fully to working in remote or hybrid modes, talent acquisition remains challenging, and employee satisfaction and retention have become key performance metrics. As companies contemplate their next PC refresh, more IT decision-makers (ITDMs), line-of-business (LOB) managers, and HR professionals have come to recognize the importance of outfitting employees with the right PCs to help them work smart, collaborate better, and protect the company’s assets and data.

This IDC white paper commissioned by Intel illuminates critical technologies, use cases, and employee benefits of refreshing your device fleet with modern PCs; explores some of the challenges IT faces, including keeping those PCs and the data they contain secure; and finally, looks at the evolving impact of employee recruitment, retainment, and satisfaction as it relates to the PCs companies deploy.

A happy, highly collaborative workforce is a critical element of a successful company, and research shows that employees receiving modern PCs get more done and are happier.

New PCs’ benefits include enhanced endpoint security, upgraded data protection capabilities, and improved manageability features. Each of these is designed to help limit employee downtime, enhance productivity, and streamline the job of IT.

Intel is driving innovation in this space with its 13th Generation Intel Core Processors, which offer high-end CPU performance features. Companies looking for best-in-class security and manageability should consider PCs that leverage the Intel vPro platform.

PCs on the Intel vPro platform are tuned for collaboration and productivity, supported by the hybrid architecture that allocates resources to the job at hand, including collaboration apps and video calls.

4 Key Takeaways

94% of ITDMs and LOB respondents said the quality of the PC they deploy was either somewhat or very important when it came to recruiting new employees or retaining existing ones.

A majority (71%) of the respondents either agreed or strongly agreed with the statement that younger millennials and Gen Z prospects are more likely to take a job if offered a modern, well-equipped PC as a new employee.

When asked about the importance of a high-quality PC when it comes to employee productivity and job satisfaction, 96% of employees said it was either somewhat or very important.

98% of respondents noted that PC-based tools have become somewhat or very important in driving better collaboration inside the company.

“In the face of challenging macroeconomic headwinds, some companies will consider delaying the refresh of their PC fleets. This would be a mistake. Modern PCs represent a critical investment in the happiness and productivity of your employees and the long-term success of your business.”
