ENGIE Digital Optimizes Cloud Cost Spend

ENGIE Digital uses Intel® Cloud Optimizer to manage cloud cost, enabling it to maintain and operate its renewable assets.

At a glance:

  • ENGIE is a group dedicated to low-carbon energy services, with over 500 wind and solar assets deployed in 25 countries. Darwin is a platform hosted in the cloud dedicated to renewable energy, specializing in maintaining and operating renewable assets.
  • Densify and Intel introduced Intel® Cloud Optimizer to help ENGIE Digital manage the challenge of increased cloud cost spend.
