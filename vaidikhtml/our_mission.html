Our Mission

Bringing AI intelligence everywhere, from edge to cloud

Our mission is to enable diverse teams with varying levels of expertise to leverage vision AI in their field. We aim to accelerate AI adoption as innovative solutions positively impact many industries, such as manufacturing, smart cities, retail, and video safety.

Intel Geti software is also part of Intel’s Edge Platform, a new solution designed to empower enterprises to build, deploy, run, and manage edge and AI solutions. With Intel’s Edge Platform, customers can implement AI models and applications in edge-to-cloud hybrid implementation at scale.

Our Vision

Empowering You Through AI

Intel® Geti™ software is a key part of Intel’s focus on empowering, enabling, and influencing others to create a better world for ourselves and future generations. AI has been at the forefront of medical, industrial, social and technological change in our society. We believe that AI can help create global changes, empower us with the right tools and enable a responsible, inclusive and sustainable future.
