Agilex™ 7 FPGA I-Series Transceiver-SoC Development Kit (4x F-Tile)

The Agilex™ 7 FPGA I-Series Transceiver SoC development kit delivers a complete design environment that includes both hardware and software for developing with Agilex™ 7 FPGA I-Series that contain 4x F-Tiles.​

Learn more about Agilex™ 7 FPGA I-Series devices ›​

You can use this development kit to:​

  • Evaluate Agilex™ 7 FPGA I-Series SoC designs with the hard processor system (HPS).
  • Evaluate F-Tile FGT and FHT transceiver performance.​
  • Generate and check pseudo-random binary sequence (PRBS) patterns.
  • Dynamically change differential output voltage (VOD) pre-emphasis and equalization settings to optimize transceiver performance for your channel.
  • Perform jitter analysis.
  • Design for flexible protocol support in Video and Vision, JESD204C, SerialLite IV, CPRI, and other major standards.
