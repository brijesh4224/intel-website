Create Irresistible Workplaces for Remote, Hybrid, and Work-from-Home Productivity

Atos is bringing multi-OS support to its Digital Workplace solutions by combining Intel vPro® platform-enabled Chromebooks and Chrome Enterprise services. This offering takes advantage of key Intel vPro® platform features:

  • Intel® Hardware Shield for below-the-OS security capabilities and Intel® Active Management Technology (Intel® AMT) for remote device manageability.
  • Intel® Endpoint Management Assistant (Intel® EMA), which extends Intel® AMT functionality over the cloud to known Wi-Fi networks beyond the corporate firewall.
  • Support for Wi-Fi 6E and resource prioritization on select devices, which helps prevent dropped packets during important video meetings.

Now businesses with managed IT can own their purchasing strategy, ensure solution fit with the current environment, and ready their dynamic workforces for frictionless productivity.
