Intel's Commitment to Open Source

We believe that open ecosystems are more powerful than closed. For decades, we have been committed to being the company that creates, democratizes, ​and enables open ecosystems. ​As a Premier member of the PyTorch* Foundation, a founding member of the OPEA project, and a contributor to the UXL Foundation, we lead AI innovation and enhance Intel® hardware with open software.

As part of our commitment to the open ecosystem, Intel pledges to:

  • Develop in the open, be inclusive in our approach, and support our communities' efforts while embracing diversity and our differences because they make us better
  • Invest in open software communities to promote open, industry-shaping standards
  • Support developers and partners with choice and collaboration
  • Deliver a software ecosystem that enables efficient and cost-effective goal achievement
