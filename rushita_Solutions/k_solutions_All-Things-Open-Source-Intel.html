Supporting Open Source for an Innovative Ecosystem

As a contributor to the open source software community since 1989, Intel uses industry collaboration, co-engineering, and open source contributions to deliver a steady stream of code and optimizations that work across multiple platforms and use cases.

Our contributions include initiatives with OPEA, PyTorch, and TensorFlow projects. Intel pushes our contributions upstream so developers get the most current and optimized software that works across multiple platforms and maintains security.

As AI grows, Intel believes that an open AI ecosystem is the only way to ensure the responsible use of AI.
