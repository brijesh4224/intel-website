Enhance Student Learning with Edtech for K‒12 Education

The importance of technology in K‒12 education cannot be overstated. Some type of edtech touches all education stakeholders every day—from school custodians to district superintendents. Even more important is edtech’s impact on teachers and students. For educators, it enables new, innovative teaching strategies and deeper personalized learning that helps unlock student potential. For students, it can spark curiosity and creativity, inspire innovative thinking, and open up new learning opportunities.

Edtech is more than just laptops and computers. It’s an entire ecosystem of devices, including edge, cloud, networking, and data center technologies, that—when they work together—can increase access, engagement, collaboration, and personalization.

With our comprehensive edtech portfolio and large ecosystem of partners worldwide, we are here to work with you, no matter if you’re just starting to implement edtech in your district or if you’re on your latest refresh cycle. Together we can create an educational technology foundation that realizes amazing outcomes for learners around the world.

Much of today’s technology was born from research in universities or created by students who pursued an education in STEAM-related fields. From AI to data centers to high performance computing, universities and students in higher education help both design and innovate new technologies that can make the world a smarter and more convenient place where technology blends into our everyday lives.

Not only does Intel partner with universities to help fuel innovation in academia, but Intel also has a vast diversity of technologies that can help bring other technological research and ideas to life. Technology’s role in higher education helps shape students’ ability to apply their knowledge to real-world careers as well as researchers’ potential to create new technological innovations.
