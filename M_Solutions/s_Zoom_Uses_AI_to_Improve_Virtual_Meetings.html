Zoom Uses AI to Improve Virtual Meetings

Zoom virtual meeting enhancements such virtual background images and background noise detection rely on AI and deep learning.

At a glance:

  • Zoom’s communications platform started with video as its foundation, and they continue set the standard for innovation.

  • Zoom introduced virtual backgrounds for mobile, laptop, and desktop devices. They have introduced advanced AI background noise detection to automatically reduce environmental noises, such as fans, dogs barking, and vacuum cleaners. All these features rely heavily on artificial intelligence and deep learning, which benefits from the Intel® oneAPI Deep Neural Network Library.
