Overview

The Intel® Certified Instructor Program endorses qualified third-party developers to teach oneAPI content. Instructors are certified following a rigorous training and evaluation process that assesses technical mastery and instructor ability.

Topic catalog:

  • MLOps Essentials
  • Machine Learning Using oneAPI
  • oneAPI C++ SYCL* Essentials
  • OpenMP* Offload Basics
  • Intel® Rendering Toolkit
  • CUDA* to C++ SYCL Migration

Certified instructors come from industry partners, academia, and national laboratories to bring their distinctive expertise and technical background into each workshop they lead.

Program Benefits

Endorsement by Intel provides instructors with:

  • Intel Certified Instructor Program badge and certificate (per topic)
  • Featured profile on the Intel Software Experts page
  • Editable Microsoft PowerPoint* presentations with speaker notes and additional instructional resources
  • Ongoing training and product updates by Intel experts

Criteria

Certified instructors must have experience in any of the following topics:

  • High-performance computing (HPC)
  • Data science
  • Computer vision
  • Machine learning
  • AI

In addition, instructors must:

  • Demonstrate technical mastery in the topic of instructor certification
  • Showcase their instructor ability through a submitted instruction sample
  • Support Intel’s cross-architecture vision using oneAPI

Annual certification renewal is required. The renewal process involves continuing education and a record of instructor activity.
