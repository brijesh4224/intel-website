How to Get the Latest Intel® Virtual RAID on CPU (Intel® VROC) Driver for Windows*

Content Type: Product Information & Documentation | Article ID: 000090439 | Last Reviewed: 09/24/2024

Description

Unable to determine where to obtain the latest Intel® VROC driver for Windows* for my specific platform.

Resolution

The Intel® VROC driver for Windows* can be downloaded from the Intel Download Center based on the Intel platform/motherboard being used, or from the website of the system manufacturer in case it is a third-party system.

Keep in mind that the Intel® VROC drivers available in the Intel Download Center are intended for Intel server systems only. If you are using a non-Intel server system, it is highly recommended that you contact your system manufacturer to get the respective Intel® VROC drivers.

Additional information

It is important that you consult with the system manufacturer about the latest available Intel® VROC driver for Windows*, as they may have a customized version that has been tested and validated to work with a specific system model. The versions available in the Intel site are specific for different Intel systems, and they may not work with third-party systems.

Are you an OEM/ODM looking for the latest Intel® VROC Software Package?OEM/ODM customers may get the latest Intel® VROC Software Package from the Intel Resource & Documentation Center (login is required). If you do not have access, refer to your respective Intel representative for assistance.
