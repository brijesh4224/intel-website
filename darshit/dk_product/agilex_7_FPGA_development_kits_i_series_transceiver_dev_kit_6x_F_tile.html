Agilex™ 7 FPGA I-Series Transceiver Development Kit (6x F-Tile)

The Agilex™ 7 FPGA I-Series Transceiver development kit delivers a complete prototyping and reference platform design environment that includes hardware and software for developing with Agilex™ 7 FPGA I-Series containing 6x F-Tiles.

Learn more about  Agilex™ 7 FPGA I-Series devices ›

You can use this development kit to:

  • Evaluate Agilex™ 7 FPGA I-Series designs (6x F Tile) transceiver capabilities.
  • Evaluate F-Tile FGT and FHT transceiver performance.
  • Generate and check pseudo-random binary sequence (PRBS) patterns.
  • Dynamically change differential output voltage (VOD) pre-emphasis and equalization settings to optimize transceiver performance for your channel.
  • Perform jitter analysis.
  • Accelerate your product design for general purpose embedded solution as well as Wireline OTN, muxponder, multiplexer, crosspoint switch, Wireless designs etc., with FMC+ connectors.
