Meet Increasing Storage Demands with Next-Generation Storage Capabilities

Store, move, and manage your data easier while freeing up your CPU for critical, demanding workloads, with Intel® Storage Engines available on Intel® Xeon® Scalable processors.

These built-in storage accelerators help reduce power consumption and deliver enhanced capabilities, such as on-the-fly compression and encryption, fast data movement, and integrated NVMe device control, at scale, for your enterprise, cloud, or edge storage.

How Your Business Benefits from Intel® Storage Engines

Increase Capacity and Performance

Offload ever-increasing storage functions from the CPU to preserve headroom for more-compute-intense workloads, boosting overall capacity and performance.

Eliminate I/O Bottlenecks and Latency

Avoid the inherent delays that occur when data is shuttled to discrete accelerators or external appliances by leveraging Intel® Storage Engines built directly into the CPU.

Reduce Complexity and Costs

Deliver storage workload acceleration in a single architecture, eliminating the need for additional hardware investments.

Simplify Workload Shifting

Shift workloads from the CPU to integrated accelerators faster and easier by making simple code changes, enabling drivers, and configuring BIOS settings.
