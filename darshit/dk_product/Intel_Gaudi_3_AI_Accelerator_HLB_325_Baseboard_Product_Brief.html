Description

The Intel® Gaudi® 3 baseboard (HLB-325) accommodates 8 Intel® Gaudi® 3 AI accelerator OAM mezzanine cards and provides customers a module subsystem that is easy to migrate into their AI server designs. The HLB-325 provides direct all-to-all connectivity with 4.2 Tera-Bytes (TB) per second of bi-directional bandwidth between the 8 Intel® Gaudi® 3 accelerators, without requiring a separate switching IC.
