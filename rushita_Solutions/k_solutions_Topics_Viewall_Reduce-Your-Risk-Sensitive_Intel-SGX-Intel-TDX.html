Description

Wherever you set the trust boundary for your business, Intel will help you protect it. Intel® Software Guard Extensions, or Intel® SGX enables the smallest trust boundary for confidential computing. It’s the most researched, updated, and deployed confidential computing technology in the data center in the market today. Intel® Trust Domain Extensions or Intel® TDX provides security at the virtual machine level. While offering both Intel SGX and Intel TDX in our confidential computing portfolio, we give customers options for how they help protect sensitive data and code while maintaining high levels of performance.
