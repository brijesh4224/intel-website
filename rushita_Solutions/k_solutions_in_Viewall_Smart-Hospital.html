Transforming Patient Care

Hospitals today are going through a digital transformation as they embrace technological advances to help improve patient care. While many of the endpoint solutions hospitals deploy bring new levels of precision and efficiency to clinical processes, integration across the hospital ecosystem and all points of care delivery—from registration to imaging, the operating room to the nurses’ station—is critical to fully realize the benefits available from a connected, intelligent hospital.

Smart hospitals are taking a holistic approach to infrastructure modernization, integrating digital and physical assets in a unified framework that ties the institution’s various clinical and business workflows and assets together. As a result, smart hospitals realize expansive data access across the health ecosystem, from hospital to clinic to provider and beyond, as well as accelerated clinical workflows, streamlined patient journeys, and the ability to deploy new lifesaving innovations as they are brought to market.

Smart Hospital Benefits

To deliver the kind of connected care patients expect from top institutions, smart hospitals enable seamless integration across technologies to aid in healthcare delivery.

Near-Real-Time Access to Data

Volumes of data generated by smart hospitals are transformed into actionable insights to inform and expedite decisions, deliver more-personalized care, and reduce costs. Data from sensors and other digital assets is fully integrated, enabling improved visibility into resource utilization and better management of fluctuating caseloads.

End-to-End Data Security

A defense-in-depth approach to security prevents system compromises, ensures patient privacy, and keeps smart hospitals in compliance with industry standards.

Connected Care across the Healthcare Network

Integration with specialty clinics, primary care providers, and other healthcare venues allows patients to receive the right care at the right time and place, anywhere in the community. Patient experiences and outcomes benefit from operational efficiencies, automated workflows, and connectivity between systems, devices, assets, data, and people.

Real-Time Computing

Real-time computing is critical in conditions requiring prompt and accurate insights on data. Intel® real-time technology, with integrated Intel® Time Coordinated Computing (Intel® TCC) features and support for Time-Sensitive Networking (TSN), enables smart hospital solutions that require a high degree of coordination, within and across networked medical and other devices.

Delivering Connected Care

A model for the future of healthcare, smart hospitals apply AI and analytics to their connected infrastructure to transform data from devices and workflows into actionable insights. This data-driven approach enables faster, more-precise diagnostics, more informed decision-making, and better patient care.

Intelligent Medical Imaging

Getting high-resolution medical imaging—CT scans, MRIs, nuclear imaging, and other imaging types—quickly into the hands of clinicians and radiologists is critical for diagnosis and treatment. High-performance edge computing solutions support near-real-time processing and analysis of these data-intensive medical imaging workloads. High bandwidth private 5G connectivity enables distributed access to data across form factors. AI helps to reveal new ways to streamline workflows, enhance performance, and improve image accuracy.

Intelligent Diagnostics and Monitoring

More intelligence in devices and at the edge is driving patient-centered, data-centric care to new levels. Advanced AI and predictive analytics applied to patient data, combined with increased speed of data sharing with 5G connectivity, deliver actionable insights to accelerate diagnoses and treatment options, improve workflow efficiency, and deliver care wherever the patient may need it.

Labs

Innovations and automation applied in smart hospital labs errors, increase efficiency, support advanced and specialized testing, and deliver faster turnaround times. System integration with the hospital’s unified technology platform gets results to clinicians faster, accelerating diagnoses and the delivery of care.

Healthcare Information Technology (IT)

Evolving today’s management of the health IT environment inside a hospital or lab system is critical for delivering and sustaining the long-term benefits offered by edge computing, AI, IoT, and more. Innovative compute platforms and flexible, extensible, and scalable IT infrastructures are needed to capture, process, transfer, and store data. Enabling performant applications and workloads to reliably work together simplifies management of the increasing flow of data and enables extraction of insights that can improve speed of service and health system productivity.

Intel Solutions for Telehealth

The demand for smart, connected, and performant telehealth solutions has never been greater. Telehealth solutions, from patient consultation applications to advanced patient monitoring systems, are improving access to care and making clinicians more efficient and productive. When they take advantage of edge analytics, cloud-based data gathering, and advanced connectivity, they can surround the patient in care from the first point of contact to the completion of treatments.

Frequently Asked Questions

A smart hospital deploys AI, IoT, 5G networks, and other technological advances from edge to cloud to seamlessly coordinate and align operations for accelerated and enhanced patient care. From registration to imaging, the operating room or the nurses’ station, the improved connectivity and breadth of data generated can deliver valuable insights to improve patient experiences, streamline clinician workflows, and reduce costs.

Seamless integration across infrastructure is fundamental to building a smart hospital. With the use of technologies like AI, IoT, and 5G networks from the edge to the cloud, achieving high levels of interoperability enables agility, broad data sharing and near-real time communications that define the smart hospital model.

Hospitals today are generating unprecedented volumes of clinical and business data, yet many lack the integrated infrastructure needed to fully optimize data in vitally important diagnostic and treatment decisions. Smart hospitals integrate assets and resources across the environment for near-real-time data access and analysis, informing and accelerating patient journeys, increasing operational efficiencies, and optimizing resource utilization for better experiences and outcomes.

Intel is the trusted leader in enabling technology that empowers healthcare use cases and the healthcare industry. With a robust partner ecosystem and vast hardware and software portfolio, Intel® technology provides the agility, scale, and design flexibility smart hospitals need to deploy new clinical solutions as they emerge—enhancing patient journeys and outcomes and driving the next generation of healthcare innovations.
