EDA Tool Support Resource Center

Resources for EDA tools supported in the Intel® Quartus® Prime Software.

The Intel® Quartus® Prime Edition Software supports interfaces with third-party EDA tools throughout the design flow. For more information about Intel® FPGA EDA partnerships, refer to the EDA Partners page.

For documentation and training on EDA tool support in the Intel® Quartus® Prime Edition Software, refer to the appropriate resource center:

  • I/O Management and Board Development Support Center
  • Synthesis and Netlist Viewers Resource Center
  • Questa*-Intel® FPGA Edition Quick-Start: Intel® Quartus® Prime Pro Edition
  • ModelSim* - Intel® FPGA Edition Simulation Quick-Start: Intel® Quartus® Prime Pro Edition

For additional information on using EDA tools with the Quartus Prime or Quartus II software, refer to the following topics from Help:

  • Creating and Instantiating Intel® Quartus® Prime IP Cores in Other EDA Tools
