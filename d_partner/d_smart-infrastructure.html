Bring Your Smart Infrastructure Project to Life

From the first roads and canals before industrialization to the national US highway system of the 1950s to the technological innovations of the 21st century, infrastructure is constantly evolving to both support and drive new economic and societal advancements. The next several decades will be no different, as new, digital smart infrastructure—which combines technological innovations, like cloud, 5G, AI, and IoT, with physical infrastructure for land, water, and air transportation—establishes a foundation from which communities, cities, and governments can create a more sustainable, affordable, safe, and equitable world.

Funding from legislation, such as the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act (IIJA) in the US, offers an opportunity to rethink and redefine what infrastructure is and can be. Delivering transformational, modern infrastructure starts with a solid strategy and plan that raises the quality of life for all citizens, from optimizing citizen outcomes to creating greater economic opportunity to achieving climate resiliency goals.

Whether you are in the initial stages of developing a smart infrastructure strategy or looking for deployment-ready digital infrastructure solutions, Intel and our partners are here to help you:

Frequently Asked Questions

Smart infrastructure combines physical infrastructure and digital technologies to enable economic competitiveness, drive climate responsibility, and provide more equitable access to public transportation and services for all citizens. This combination—where silicon and software complement concrete and steel—more effectively meets the needs of our modern, information-driven economy today while enabling a path for upgrades over time as technology evolves. Smart infrastructure has the potential to foster unprecedented outcomes such as reduced traffic fatalities, a significant decrease in urban traffic congestion that could save billions of pounds of CO2 emissions 1, and exponential economic growth.
