Edge-Native Security Solution for Utilities

Threat Sense at the Edge, a cloud-to-edge platform from SAP and Intel, uses AI to give energy and utility companies new levels of substation security and operational insights. The as-a-service solution leverages SAP’s Digital Core and the edge-native software infrastructure of the Intel® Tiber™ Edge Platform to rapidly sort through data and recommend the appropriate level and type of response to events as they happen.
