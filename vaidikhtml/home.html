Get started building vision models with Intel® Geti™ software

What is Intel® Geti™ Software?

Intel’s new software for building computer vision models in a fraction of the time and with less data. This software eases laborious data labeling, model training and optimization tasks across the AI model development process, empowering teams to produce custom AI models at scale.

Scaling Edge AI Solutions with Intel

Intel Geti software is part of the Intel® Tiber™ Edge Platform, a solution designed to solve edge challenges across industries. The platform enables enterprises to develop, deploy, run, manage, and scale edge applications with cloud-like simplicity, leveraging an unmatched partner ecosystem.

The Edge Platform is a modular solution that adapts to customer needs, offering industry solutions, AI & apps, and infrastructure software. Intel Geti software is a crucial component in the AI and Applications category, utilized for building vision AI models.
