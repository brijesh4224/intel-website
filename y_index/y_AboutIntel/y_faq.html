Intel's stock symbol is INTC

Intel Common stock CUSIP is 458140-10-0

When was Intel's initial public offering and the IPO price?

Intel went public October 13, 1971 at a price of USD 23.50 per share or 2 cents adjusted for our 13 subsequent stock splits.

What year and place was Intel Corporation incorporated?

Intel was incorporated 3/1/89 in Delaware. Below is the history from Intel California to the current Intel Delaware:

7/18/68 Incorporated: California, as NM Electronics Inc.
8/6/68 Name changed to Intel Corporation, a California Corporation.
3/1/89 Incorporation of Intel Corporation, a Delaware Corporation.
5/5/89 Intel Corporation (California) merged with and into Intel Corporation (Delaware). Intel Corporation (Delaware) is the surviving corporation.

Who is Intel's accounting firm?

Ernst & Young LLP. They have been Intel's auditors since the company's inception in 1968.
