Achieve Your Goals with a Strategic Ally and Trusted Public Sector Solutions

Public sector agencies and organizations have the formidable task of continuously delivering on large-scale operational goals and initiatives in environments with ever-changing conditions and requirements.

Accomplishing your missions requires secure, flexible, cost-effective infrastructure and systems that can adapt to shifting needs; intelligent technology and reliable connectivity for near-real-time information and insights; and computing power to drive new discoveries.

We are dedicated to helping you succeed with technologies, solutions, and expert support that accelerate the deployment of security-enabled government technology solutions. Our substantial investments in innovation, open source software, AI, and 5G technologies and partner collaborations help us deliver solutions you can trust—from our safe, secure supply chain to hardware-based security capabilities that do not compromise performance.

For command, control, communications, and computers, Intel is your partner every step of the way, enabling you to make more-informed decisions, keep mission-critical systems and data secure, and improve the quality of life for all citizens.
