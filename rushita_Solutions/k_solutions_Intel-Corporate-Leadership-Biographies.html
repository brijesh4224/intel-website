Executive Leadership Team

Interim co-CEO: David (Dave) Zinsner – David Zinsner is interim co-CEO, executive vice president and chief financial officer at Intel.

Interim co-CEO: Michelle Johnston Holthaus – Michelle Johnston Holthaus is interim co-CEO and CEO of Intel Products at Intel Corporation.

April Miller Boise – April Miller Boise is executive vice president, chief legal officer and corporate secretary of Intel Corporation.

Naga Chandrasekaran – Naga Chandrasekaran is executive vice president and chief global operations officer at Intel Corporation.

Keyvan Esfarjani – Keyvan Esfarjani is executive vice president and chief global operations officer at Intel Corporation.

Justin Hotard – Justin Hotard is executive vice president and general manager of the Data Center and AI Group at Intel.

Sachin Katti – Sachin Katti is senior vice president and general manager of the Network and Edge Group.

Dr. Ann B. Kelleher – Dr. Ann B. Kelleher is executive vice president and general manager of Foundry Technology Development at Intel Corporation.

Greg Lavender – Greg Lavender is executive vice president, chief technology officer and GM of the Office of the Chief Technology Officer.

Kevin O’Buckley – Kevin O’Buckley is senior vice president and general manager of Foundry Services at Intel Corporation.

Christy Pambianchi – Christy Pambianchi is executive vice president and chief people officer at Intel Corporation.

Matt Poirier – Matt Poirier is senior vice president of Corporate Development at Intel Corporation.

Sandra L. Rivera – Sandra L. Rivera is chief executive officer of Altera, an Intel Company.

Christoph Schell – Christoph Schell is executive vice president and chief commercial officer at Intel Corporation.

Safroadu (Saf) Yeboah-Amankwah – Safroadu (Saf) Yeboah-Amankwah is senior vice president and chief strategy officer (CSO) at Intel Corporation.

About Intel

Intel (Nasdaq: INTC) is an industry leader, creating world-changing technology that enables global progress and enriches lives. Inspired by Moore’s Law, we continuously work to advance the design and manufacturing of semiconductors to help address our customers’ greatest challenges.
