Highest Bandwidth Transceiver Technology1

Transceivers in FPGA devices are essential for high-speed data communication, data serialization/deserialization, protocol adaptation, error handling, and various other tasks in applications that demand high performance, low latency, and flexibility. They enable FPGAs to serve as versatile, high-speed data processing and communication platforms in a wide range of industries.

Agilex™ 7 FPGA with High Performance 116G Transceivers

Agilex™ 7 FPGA has the industry's first 116Gbps PAM4 multi-channel and multi-protocol transceivers built into the devices.

Intel's mid-range FPGA series enables high-performance and low-latency FPGA designs due to its integration into the fabric. These FPGAs are perfect for embedded applications requiring small form factor.
