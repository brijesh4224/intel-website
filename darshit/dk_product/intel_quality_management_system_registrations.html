1. Intel ISO 9001:2015 Registrations

Intel Quality Management System Registrations

Intel achieves certification to the internationally recognized ISO 9001 standard to ensure our sites maintain a comprehensive quality management system. This ensures Intel products and services meet the needs of customers through an effective quality management system.

The ISO certification is the recognition of Intel’s commitment to consistent product quality and customer satisfaction, and our dedication to continuous improvement.

Intel Wafer Fabrication and Assembly and Test sites, Post Sales Support, Intel Stress Labs, and Intel Programmable Solutions Group are registered to the ISO 9001 standard by a third-party registrar.
