Build with Intel

Intel is enabling choice and flexibly for the ecosystem.

  • Intel has a diverse portfolio of silicon, CPUs, Accelerators, Ethernet and storage. And all of this has been optimized for the Edge.
  • In addition to that, we have a robust set of developer tools, tools that are driving the convergence of analytics and inference, media optimizations as well as a variety of networking workloads, all of this coming together to enable vertical-specific offerings. (e.g. OpenVINO – for AI Deep Learning Inferencing, OpenNESS which is being used for Networking and 5G Capabilities, Open Visual Cloud for media enhancements, DevCloud).

The Power of AI and Edge Computing in Achieving Defect-Free Factories

  • Most manufacturers would agree that one of their top priorities is producing high quality products. This can be challenging due to a number of factors, such as time contstraints, cost of inspection steps, or lack of consistency in manual inspection processes.
  • Edge computing is bringing AI compute closer to the origin of data, closer to the mass of sensors connected to the machines, and closer to devices on the factory floor.
  • Intel will discuss how an advanced analytics pipeline combined with the power of AI can achieve significant benefits for Industrial IoT companies and their customers.
