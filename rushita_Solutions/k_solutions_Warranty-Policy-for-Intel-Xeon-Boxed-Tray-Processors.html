Warranty Terms and Conditions for Intel® Products

Content Type: Warranty & RMA | Article ID: 000100348 | Last Reviewed: 04/22/2025

Intel® Xeon® Processors are sold as either tray or boxed. Refer to What Is the Difference Between Boxed and Tray Processors?

Limited Warranty Policy for Intel® Xeon® Boxed Processor

Intel offers a three-year limited warranty with boxed processors that can be fulfilled by Intel (some are offered with a one-year limited warranty). The warranty eligibility period starts on the original purchase date and doesn't reset if Intel provides a product replacement.

Limited Warranty Policy for Intel® Xeon® Tray Processors

  • For resellers, integrators, and end users, the warranty is provided at your place of purchase.  Intel offers a one-year limited warranty solely to direct customers. 
  • When Intel® Xeon® processors are included in systems from original equipment manufacturers (OEMs), they fall under the OEMs manufacturer warranties, and Intel does not provide direct support. For warranty assistance, contact your OEM or distributor.
  • Starting with Intel® Xeon® W-2400 and Intel® Xeon® W-3400 processors, Intel offers a three-year limited warranty with tray processors solely to direct customers. For prior-generation Intel® Xeon® W processors, Intel generally offers a one-year limited warranty solely to direct customers.
  • Tray processors pre-installed in Intel® Data Center Systems (Intel® DCS) offer a three-year limited warranty. Learn more in this product brief.

How to check the warranty status of Intel® Xeon® Processors and whether it is tray or boxed

  1. Go to Warranty Information.
  2. Select Processors on Product Type.
  3. Enter batch number (FPO).
  4. Enter full serial number (ATPO) or partial serial number (ATPO).
  5. Click Check products.

For FPO and ATPO numbers, refer to How to Identify My Intel® Xeon® Processor Markings for Warranty Requests.
