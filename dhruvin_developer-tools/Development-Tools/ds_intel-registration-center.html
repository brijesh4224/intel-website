Entitlement

Manage all software and service entitlements issued by Intel from the Intel Registration Center (IRC) User Portal. The entitlement defines your rights to use the products and services, and the period for which it is valid. From the entitlement, you can also identify other services and support that you may receive.

License

Any software that requires a license (also known as license key) for your entitlements can be found here. The license is generated from your entitlement after it is activated. Additionally, the IRC User Portal allows you to manage multiple users accessing the muti-user licenses, or download a license file when it is needed for installing the software, or transferring the license to a different system.

Downloads

The IRC User Portal provides downloads for all of your entitled products. The product documentation is available along with the product downloads for your convenience.
