Smoother Gaming with AI-Generated Frames

Intel Xe Frame Generation technology enhances your gameplay with added fluidity. AI-generated frames are interlaced between existing graphics output to significantly increase the number of frames per second (FPS) and boost performance.

Intel Xe Low Latency

Faster Response Time

Intel® Xe Low Latency technology makes you feel more connected to the game by reducing latency from your keyboard and mouse to what happens on screen.

Advanced algorithms predict an optimal path from user inputs to screen frame output to make you feel like what you’re controlling on-screen is directly connected to you.2
