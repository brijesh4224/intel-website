About this offer

WaitTime is a patented, real-time and historical artificial intelligence software that uses state-of-the-art imaging techniques to monitor crowd behavior in large scale venues. The data generated from WaitTime software leverages operations and guest engagement platforms to enable real-time smart decision making, inform operations management, and engage guests in movement and distribution solutions.
