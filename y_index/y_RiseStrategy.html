Our Roadmap for Climate Action

Our Climate Transition Action Plan outlines our commitment to integrating sustainability into our core business, operations, value chain, and products. Our research teams are thinking a decade or more into the future.

Our ESG Framework and RISE Strategy

We have formalized our governance structure so that Intel's environmental, social, and governance (ESG) efforts are integrated throughout our business. Through our unified “One Intel” ESG framework, RISE, we aim to create a more responsible, inclusive, and sustainable world, enabled by technology and the expertise and passion of our employees.

Responsible

We have a long history as a leader in advancing safety, wellness, and responsible business practices across our operations, our industry, our value chain, and beyond.

$27 Million in Fees Remediated

We set expectations with our suppliers that workers should not have to pay for their employment. As a result of our efforts, suppliers in our global supply chain have returned more than $27 million in fees to their workers since 2014.

Sustainable

We have long worked to reduce the environmental impact of our own operations, while also collaborating with others to help solve the world’s environmental challenges.

99% Renewable Electricity Across Our Operations

In 2023, we achieved 100% renewable electricity in the US, European Union, Israel, Malaysia, Vietnam, and China, and are approaching 100% in Costa Rica—bringing the global total to 99%.

Read about climate and energy initiatives

Transformation Through Sustainable AI

We believe in a sustainable future for—and through—artificial intelligence (AI). Intel has taken on the challenge to reduce energy consumption with AI workloads through comprehensive measures, including platform innovations.

Learn how we are helping drive sustainable AI

Reaching Net Positive Water

In 2023, we reached net positive water in four countries: the US, India, Costa Rica, and Mexico, meaning that water returned through water management practices plus water restored to local watersheds was equivalent to >100% of our fresh water consumption.

Read more about water stewardship at Intel

Intel and the Polish Ministry of Agriculture have collaborated to roll out Intel’s AI for Future Workforce program to vocational schools. The program brings cutting-edge AI skills to future agriculture workers in Poland, one of the biggest agricultural suppliers in the European Union.
