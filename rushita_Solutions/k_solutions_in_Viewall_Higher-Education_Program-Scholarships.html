Intel-Sponsored Scholarships Available

Every year, Intel, and its partners award hundreds of scholarships to support students pursuing an undergraduate or graduate degree in STEM. Intel Scholars also receive impactful career-building opportunities, including an Intel mentorship program and exposure to Intel job opportunities.

Applications, with awards of $5,000 to $10,000 for African American, Latinx, Native American, women, and veteran students pursuing an undergraduate or graduate degree in STEM, are now closed.

We work closely with our partners to manage the application and review process, and issue funding to awardees. Eligibility requirements are subject to change. Please click the partner links below for more details.
