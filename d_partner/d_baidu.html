Why Intel?

Accelerated AI

Baidu solutions take advantage of Intel optimizations to deliver fast AI performance. With Intel® Xeon® Scalable processors and Intel® Deep Learning Boost (Intel® DL Boost), Baidu PaddlePaddle 2.0 achieved a 3.53x performance increase1 for image classification.

Hardware-Enabled Security

Intel® processors with Intel® Software Guard Extensions (Intel® SGX) provide data isolation in memory to help secure workloads in the Baidu AI Cloud, and help enable confidential computing when linked with the Baidu PaddlePaddle deep learning platform.

Industry-Leading Technology

Baidu collaborates with Intel to integrate the latest technologies into AI, cloud, and data center infrastructure services. Customers benefit from key optimizations in performance that help lower costs while accelerating deployment and time to results.

Collaboration for Advanced AI

Intel® hardware and software solutions enable fast and powerful AI machine learning and deep learning from Baidu while reducing complexity and computational requirements.
