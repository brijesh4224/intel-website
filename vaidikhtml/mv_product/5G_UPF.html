About this offer

Napatech’s SmartNIC-based offload solution for 5G User Plane Function (UPF) maximizes the user capacity of packet core infrastructure. By leveraging Napatech’s solution, 5G core software vendors maximize the performance of their UPF subsystem, delivering industry-leading users-per-server capacity through the complete offload of the UPF data plane. The solution supports 140 million concurrent flows and 85 million packets per second throughput on a single SmartNIC, minimizing the cost-per-user for service providers and enterprises deploying telecom infrastructure and private networks.
