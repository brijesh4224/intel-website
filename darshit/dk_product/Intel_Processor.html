Making It Easy to Connect, Learn, and Play – Anywhere

Connect, learn, and play with the performance you need at the affordability you want on the newest Intel® Processor. Whether you’re a Chrome OS, Windows OS, or Linux OS user, you can experience an unmatched balance of next-level performance and value for the essential, entry level PC.

Features and Performance

Affordable Performance

Enjoy better multitasking while navigating various learning tools with great performance and graphics at an affordable price. Students and teachers can focus more—with fast web browsing and online applications, and worksheet calculations—for uninterrupted classroom learning.

Collaborate and Learn from Anywhere

Experience seamless collaboration. The Intel® Processor with integrated Intel® Wi-Fi 6E (Gig+) offers the best Wi-Fi technology for videoconferencing1 and education2. Next-gen Intel® UHD Graphics, an enhanced Intel® Image Processing Unit (Intel® IPU 6), and integrated MIPI cameras help you collaborate with confidence.

Watch and Play on the Go

Intel® processors feature next gen Intel® UHD graphics for HD local video playback without having to recharge. You don’t need to over-spend to enjoy long battery life and an immersive experience while watching videos or playing casual games.

Faster & More Reliable Connectivity

With ultra-fast Intel® Wi-Fi 6E (Gig+), the Intel® Processor offers nearly 3X faster connectivity for a fast and reliable connection when browsing, streaming, or collaborating3. Reduce bandwidth competition with access to an exclusive high-speed channel that other devices can’t use for a good connection in class, at home, or wherever you are.4
